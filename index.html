Ideas: More AI-resilient biosecurity with the Paraphrase Project

The Paraphrase Project, detailed in this Microsoft Research podcast, uncovered a "zero day" vulnerability where AI tools could redesign toxic protein sequences, like ricin, to bypass existing biosecurity screening systems. A cross-sector team then employed a cybersecurity-style "red-teaming" approach to develop and deploy more robust screening "patches" to synthesis companies globally through the International Gene Synthesis Consortium (IGSC). This collaborative effort significantly strengthened global biosecurity against AI-enabled threats and pioneered a novel "perpetual gatekeeping" strategy for responsibly sharing sensitive dual-use research data, balancing open science with risk mitigation.
