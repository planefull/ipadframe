Tell me when: Building agents that can wait, monitor, and act

Modern LLM agents struggle with long-running monitoring tasks like tracking emails or price drops because they lack the ability to efficiently wait and know *when* to check for updates. To address this, Microsoft Research introduced SentinelStep, a mechanism that enables agents to complete these tasks by wrapping them in a workflow with dynamic polling and careful context management. This approach significantly improves agent reliability for extended durations, paving the way for more proactive, efficient, and "always-on" AI assistants.

iOS 26.1 Features: Everything New in iOS 26.1

iOS 26.1 is the first major update to the iOS 26 software, now available for everyone with new features and fixes. Key improvements include a transparency toggle for Liquid Glass, the ability to disable the Lock Screen camera swipe, and a "Slide to Stop" bar for alarms and timers. Additionally, the update expands Apple Intelligence and AirPods Live Translation languages, brings various UI tweaks across apps, and for iPadOS, reintroduces Slide Over multitasking.
