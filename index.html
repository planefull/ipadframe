MMCTAgent: Enabling multimodal reasoning over large video and image collections

Modern multimodal AI models struggle with complex real-world reasoning over long-form videos and large image collections due to limitations in temporal reasoning, cross-modal grounding, and iterative refinement. To address this, Microsoft Research developed MMCTAgent (Multi-modal Critical Thinking Agent), an open-source framework built on AutoGen that uses a Planner–Critic architecture. This design enables MMCTAgent to perform structured, iterative reasoning, leveraging specialized tools for image and video analysis to significantly enhance performance and provide explainable insights across diverse multimodal tasks.
