Ideas: More AI-resilient biosecurity with the Paraphrase Project

The Paraphrase Project uncovered a critical "zero-day" vulnerability where AI tools could redesign toxic proteins to evade current biosecurity screening systems. A collaborative, cross-sector team then developed and implemented robust "patches" to these screening methods, which were shared globally to strengthen defenses against AI-enabled misuse. This pioneering effort also established a novel "perpetual gatekeeping" strategy for responsibly sharing sensitive scientific information, underscoring the ongoing need for vigilance and cooperation in managing AI's dual-use risks in biotechnology.
