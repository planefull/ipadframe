Tell me when: Building agents that can wait, monitor, and act

Modern LLM agents struggle with long-running monitoring tasks, such as tracking emails or price changes, because they lack the ability to intelligently determine *when* to check conditions, leading to inefficiency or failure. To address this, Microsoft Research introduces SentinelStep, a mechanism implemented in Magentic-UI that enables agents to complete these tasks by wrapping them in a workflow with dynamic polling intervals and careful context management. This approach significantly improves agent reliability for monitoring tasks lasting hours or days, paving the way for more practical, proactive, and always-on intelligent assistants.
