Tell me when: Building agents that can wait, monitor, and act

Modern LLM agents struggle with long-running monitoring tasks like tracking emails or price changes because they lack the ability to intelligently "wait," often failing due to inefficient polling or context overflow. To address this, Microsoft Research developed SentinelStep, a mechanism that enables agents to perform such tasks for extended periods by dynamically adjusting polling intervals and managing context effectively. Integrated into Magentic-UI and open-sourced, SentinelStep significantly improves agent reliability for long-duration monitoring, paving the way for more patient, proactive, and resource-efficient AI assistants.

iOS 26.1 Coming Next Week: All the New Features

Apple is nearing the release of iOS 26.1, the first major update to iOS 26, which is currently in beta testing. This update introduces numerous new features and interface changes, such as a Liquid Glass transparency toggle, the ability to disable Lock Screen camera swipe, slide-to-stop alarms, and expanded language support for Apple Intelligence and AirPods Live Translation.
