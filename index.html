Ideas: More AI-resilient biosecurity with the Paraphrase Project

The article discusses the "Paraphrase Project," which uncovered a critical biosecurity vulnerability where AI tools could redesign toxic proteins to bypass existing screening systems used by DNA synthesis companies. A cross-sector team, including Microsoft Research and Twist Bioscience, developed an AI-resilient screening solution and implemented it globally, akin to a cybersecurity "zero-day" patch. This project also established a novel "perpetual gatekeeping" strategy for sensitive data, demonstrating a responsible approach to managing information hazards in dual-use AI and biotechnology research.

M5 MacBook Air Coming Spring 2026 With M5 Mac Studio and Mac Mini in Development

Apple plans to launch M5 MacBook Air models in spring 2026, alongside M5 Pro and M5 Max MacBook Pro models earlier that year, with these initial updates focused on chip upgrades rather than design changes. A significant design refresh for the MacBook Pro, introducing thinner, lighter OLED touch screens, is anticipated for late 2026 or early 2027. Furthermore, Apple is developing new M5-equipped Mac Studio and Mac mini models, as well as two new external displays.
