Applicability vs. job displacement: further notes on our recent research on AI and occupations

Microsoft Research clarified its paper on AI's occupational implications, stating it identifies where AI chatbots might be useful for tasks, not that they will eliminate jobs. The research found AI is most applicable to knowledge work and communication tasks such as writing and information gathering, helping with specific subtasks rather than physical ones. The authors emphasize the study's methodological limitations and advocate for finding a balance where AI complements human strengths rather than replacing entire occupations.
