Set Alarm-Style iPhone Reminders in iOS 26.2

iOS 26.2 introduces an optional "urgent notification system" for the Reminders app, allowing important tasks to trigger full-screen alarms similar to a wake-up call, complete with snooze and slide-to-stop functions. Users can enable this feature and create urgent reminders, which will sound even when the device is muted or a Focus is active. Additionally, there's an option to replace the snooze button with a "Complete" button, allowing for instant task completion directly from the alarm screen.
