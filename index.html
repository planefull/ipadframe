Apple's 2026 Mac Plans

Apple is planning a major refresh of its Mac lineup throughout 2026, with most models, including the MacBook Air, MacBook Pro, Mac mini, Mac Studio, and iMac, slated to receive M5 chip upgrades. The company will also introduce a new low-cost MacBook powered by the A18 Pro chip in early 2026. Furthermore, 2026 could see the debut of an innovative OLED MacBook Pro with M6 chips and a touchscreen, alongside new mini-LED Mac displays.
