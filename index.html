Crescent library brings privacy to digital identity systems

Digital identities, while convenient, introduce significant privacy risks through "linkability," where hidden identifiers allow separate credential uses to be tracked and linked to individuals. To counter this, Microsoft Research has released Crescent, a cryptographic library that adds "unlinkability" to widely used digital identity formats such as JSON Web Tokens and mobile driver's licenses. Crescent achieves this by leveraging zero-knowledge proofs and a two-stage process, enabling users to selectively disclose information and prove facts about their credentials without revealing underlying data or allowing their separate interactions to be connected.

Aqara Unveils HomeKit-Compatible Doorbell Camera G400 and Matter Hub M200

Aqara has announced several new HomeKit and Matter-compatible smart home products, expanding its ecosystem. Key new devices include the Doorbell Camera G400, offering 2K resolution and HomeKit Secure Video, and the Hub M200, which functions as a Matter Controller, Thread Border Router, and Matter bridge. Additionally, Aqara introduced specific plugs and accessories for the European and UK markets, such as a smart radiator thermostat and wall outlets.
