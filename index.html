Ideas: More AI-resilient biosecurity with the Paraphrase Project

The Paraphrase Project uncovered a critical biosecurity vulnerability where AI tools could redesign toxic protein sequences to bypass existing DNA synthesis screening systems, posing a "zero day" threat. A cross-sector team rapidly developed and deployed "patches" to strengthen these screening tools, making them more resilient against AI-generated variants of hazardous proteins. This initiative not only enhanced global biosecurity but also pioneered a "perpetual gatekeeping" strategy for scientific publications to responsibly share sensitive dual-use research, setting a precedent for future dual-use dilemmas.
