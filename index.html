Using AI to assist in rare disease diagnosis

Genetic professionals face significant challenges in diagnosing rare genetic diseases, as interpreting vast whole genome sequencing data is time-consuming and often yields no initial diagnosis. To address this, Microsoft Research, Drexel University, and the Broad Institute collaboratively studied and designed a generative AI assistant. This AI tool aims to streamline data synthesis, flag cases for reanalysis with new scientific findings, and aggregate crucial genetic information, ultimately boosting diagnostic success and reducing diagnosis time for rare conditions.
