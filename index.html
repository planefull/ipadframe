Tell me when: Building agents that can wait, monitor, and act

Modern LLM agents struggle with long-running monitoring tasks like watching for emails or price drops because they lack the ability to intelligently determine *when* to check for updates, leading to inefficiency or failure. To address this, Microsoft Research introduces SentinelStep, a mechanism that wraps agents in a workflow with dynamic polling and context management, allowing them to patiently monitor conditions for extended periods. This approach, implemented in Magentic-UI, significantly improves agent reliability for tasks lasting hours or days, laying the groundwork for more proactive and efficient AI assistants.

Here Are Apple's Release Notes for macOS Tahoe 26.1

Apple has released the Release Candidate version of macOS Tahoe 26.1 to developers and public beta testers, with a public launch anticipated next week. This update includes new features such as AutoMix support over AirPlay and improved FaceTime audio quality in poor connection conditions. Additionally, it introduces a new tinted look for Liquid Glass and enables communication safety and web content filters by default for existing child accounts aged 13-17.
