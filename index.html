Apple and OpenAI Must Face xAI Lawsuit Over Siri Integration

A Texas District Judge has ruled that Apple and OpenAI must face a lawsuit filed by Elon Musk's xAI startup, denying their bid for dismissal. xAI claims Apple and OpenAI are conspiring to dominate the AI market by exclusively integrating ChatGPT into Siri, thereby disadvantaging competing AI chatbots like Grok. The lawsuit alleges this arrangement harms competition, limits user choice, and seeks to halt their "anticompetitive scheme" while recovering billions in damages.
