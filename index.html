MMCTAgent: Enabling multimodal reasoning over large video and image collections

Microsoft Research developed MMCTAgent (Multi-modal Critical Thinking Agent) to overcome current AI limitations in complex reasoning over long-form video and large image collections, which traditional models struggle to process. Built on AutoGen, MMCTAgent uses a Planner-Critic architecture, enabling iterative reasoning, tool-based analysis, and self-evaluation to refine answers for factual accuracy and consistency. This system features specialized agents like VideoAgent and ImageAgent that integrate structured ingestion, retrieval, and fine-grained visual tools, significantly enhancing performance on multimodal benchmarks.

Apple Intelligence Apparently Too Smart for M1 Macs After Listing Error
