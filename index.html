Applicability vs. job displacement: further notes on our recent research on AI and occupations

Microsoft Research clarified its paper, "Working with AI: Measuring the Occupational Implications of Generative AI," which was misinterpreted as predicting job elimination. The original research found that AI chatbots are most useful for knowledge-based tasks like writing and information gathering, helping with subtasks in certain occupations. The authors emphasize that the study identifies where AI can assist workflows, not replace entire jobs, and caution against over-interpreting findings due to methodological limitations.

Apple Releases Fifth Public Betas of iOS 26 and More

Apple has released the fifth public betas of iOS 26, iPadOS 26, macOS 26 (Tahoe), and tvOS 26, allowing public testers to experience these updates ahead of their September launch. These betas introduce a new "Liquid Glass" design aesthetic, emphasizing transparency, subtle animations, and a more rounded appearance across interfaces. Key new features include various Apple Intelligence capabilities, Live Translation, enhanced Phone and Messages app functions, an overhauled Spotlight for macOS, and a range of other improvements in apps like Apple Music, CarPlay, and Safari.
