Using AI to assist in rare disease diagnosis

Microsoft Research, in collaboration with Drexel University and the Broad Institute, conducted a study exploring how generative AI can assist genetic professionals in diagnosing rare genetic diseases. The research identified key challenges like information overload, inefficient collaborative sharing, and difficulties in prioritizing reanalysis of unsolved cases in whole genome sequencing. They co-designed an AI assistant prototype to address these by flagging cases for reanalysis and synthesizing crucial gene and variant data from scientific literature, aiming to streamline the diagnostic process and improve patient outcomes.

Amazon Unveils New Echo Speakers with Alexa+ AI and Omnisense Technology

Amazon has launched new Echo Dot Max, Echo Studio, Echo Show 8, and Echo Show 11 smart speakers, all integrated with the new Alexa+ AI assistant and a custom Omnisense sensor platform for ambient AI capabilities. These updated devices feature faster chips, redesigned aesthetics with 3D knit fabric, and enhanced audio or display experiences, with the Echo Show models also gaining an improved camera. Available for pre-order now, these speakers aim to directly compete with Apple's HomePod and will offer early access to Alexa+, with prices ranging from $100 to $220.
