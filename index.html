Using AI to assist in rare disease diagnosis

Genetic professionals face significant challenges in diagnosing rare diseases due to the vast and complex data from whole genome sequencing, leading to low initial diagnosis rates and lengthy reanalysis processes. Microsoft Research, in collaboration with Drexel University and the Broad Institute, conducted a study to design a generative AI-based assistant to support these professionals. This AI assistant aims to streamline workflows by synthesizing biomedical data, flagging cases for reanalysis based on new findings, and facilitating collaboration, ultimately improving diagnostic yields and patient outcomes for rare genetic diseases.
