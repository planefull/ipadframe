Crescent library brings privacy to digital identity systems

Digital identities offer convenience but present privacy risks, particularly "linkability," where hidden identifiers allow various online activities to be tracked and associated with a single person. To address this, Microsoft Research has developed Crescent, a cryptographic library that introduces "unlinkability" to common identity formats like JSON Web Tokens and mobile driver's licenses. Crescent uses efficient zero-knowledge proofs to enable users to selectively disclose information and prove facts about their credentials without revealing any linking data or requiring extensive changes to existing infrastructure.

Apple Slashes 2025 MLS Season Pass to as Low as $25

Apple has significantly discounted the annual MLS Season Pass for the remainder of the 2025 season, offering it at $29, or $25 for Apple TV+ subscribers, down from the regular $99. This reduced rate covers all live and on-demand matches through the playoffs, along with features like "MLS 360" and "Sunday Night Soccer." Consistent with previous years, the annual pass will automatically renew at the standard $99 price for the 2026 season unless canceled manually.
