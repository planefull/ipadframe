Ideas: More AI-resilient biosecurity with the Paraphrase Project

The "Paraphrase Project" investigated a "zero day" vulnerability where AI tools could redesign toxic proteins to bypass existing biosecurity screening systems, as demonstrated with ricin. A cross-sector team developed red-teaming approaches and implemented "patches" to strengthen these screening tools, sharing the improved methods globally. This effort established a novel "perpetual gatekeeping" strategy for responsibly sharing sensitive research data, setting a precedent for balancing open science with minimizing information hazards in dual-use AI and biotechnology.
