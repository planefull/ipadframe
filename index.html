When industry knowledge meets PIKE-RAG: The innovation behind Signify’s customer service boost

Signify, a leading LED lighting company, faced significant challenges in providing accurate and efficient answers to professional users due to a vast array of complex product documentation in diverse formats. To address this, Signify collaborated with Microsoft Research Asia to integrate PIKE-RAG technology into their knowledge management system on Microsoft Azure. This advanced RAG system improved answer accuracy by 12% through multimodal document parsing, an end-to-end knowledge loop, and dynamic multi-hop reasoning, demonstrating strong generalization capabilities for complex industrial scenarios.

Apple Shares Liquid Glass Design Gallery

Apple is promoting its new Liquid Glass design in iOS 26 by showcasing how third-party developers are implementing the aesthetic. On its developer website, Apple features a visual gallery demonstrating these Liquid Glass experiences across apps for iPhone, iPad, Apple Watch, and Mac. The gallery highlights design changes such as eliminated bottom navigation bars, new sliders, buttons, and popovers, often comparing them to their iOS 18 counterparts.
