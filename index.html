Apple and OpenAI Must Face xAI Lawsuit Over Siri Integration

A Texas judge denied Apple and OpenAI's request to dismiss a lawsuit filed by Elon Musk's xAI startup, meaning the case will proceed. xAI sued the companies in August 2025, accusing them of conspiring to monopolize the AI market by exclusively integrating OpenAI's ChatGPT with Siri. xAI claims this partnership unfairly disadvantages rival chatbots like its Grok, limiting user choice and access to vital data, and seeks to end the alleged anti-competitive practices and recover damages.
