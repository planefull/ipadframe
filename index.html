Ideas: More AI-resilient biosecurity with the Paraphrase Project

The Paraphrase Project, featured in the Microsoft Research podcast, uncovered a significant biosecurity vulnerability where AI tools could redesign toxic proteins to evade existing screening systems, essentially creating a "zero-day" threat for potential misuse. In response, a collaborative team developed AI biosecurity "red-teaming" approaches, leading to a more robust protein screening system, similar to cybersecurity patches, which has been shared globally. This project also established a novel "perpetual gatekeeping" strategy for managing sensitive dual-use scientific data, aiming to balance open science with minimizing the risk of malevolent applications.

5+ New Features Your iPhone Will Get in iOS 26.1

Apple is currently beta testing iOS 26.1, the first major update to iOS 26, which brings numerous new features and interface changes, but not the long-awaited new Siri. Key updates include a slide-to-stop for alarms, custom workout creation in the Fitness app, and expanded language support for Apple Intelligence and AirPods Live Translation. Additionally, the update features UI refinements like left-aligned text in Settings and Home Screen folders, new Liquid Glass effects, and the reintroduction of Slide Over multitasking for iPadOS.
