iOS 26.2 Lets iPhone Users in Japan Replace Siri With Third-Party Assistants on Side Button

The iOS 26.2 beta update confirms that iPhone users in Japan will soon be able to replace Siri with a third-party voice assistant when long-pressing the Side Button. This new functionality allows users to assign alternatives like Alexa or Gemini, provided developers adopt the necessary App Intents framework. This change, along with support for third-party App Stores, is being implemented to comply with Japan's Mobile Software Competition Act Guidelines.
