Tell me when: Building agents that can wait, monitor, and act

Modern LLM agents struggle with long-running monitoring tasks, such as tracking emails or price changes, because they lack the ability to intelligently determine *when* to check, leading to inefficiency and context overflow. To address this, Microsoft Research introduced SentinelStep, a mechanism integrated into their Magentic-UI agentic system. SentinelStep enables agents to patiently monitor conditions for extended periods by dynamically adjusting polling intervals and managing context, significantly improving task reliability over hours or days.
