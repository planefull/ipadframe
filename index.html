Apple's 2026 Mac Plans

Apple is planning a significant refresh of its Mac lineup throughout 2026, primarily introducing M5 chips across most models, with a possibility of M6 chips appearing by year-end. Updates are expected for the MacBook Air, MacBook Pro, Mac mini, and Mac Studio, alongside the debut of a new, lower-cost MacBook featuring an A18 Pro chip. Additionally, two new Mac Displays are in development, and a redesigned OLED MacBook Pro with a touchscreen and M6 chip is rumored for late 2026.
