Tell me when: Building agents that can wait, monitor, and act

Modern LLM agents struggle with long-running monitoring tasks because they lack the ability to efficiently wait and check for specific conditions without excessive polling or context overflow. To address this, Microsoft Research has developed SentinelStep, a mechanism that enables agents to complete such tasks by dynamically adjusting polling intervals and managing context effectively. Implemented in their Magentic-UI prototype, SentinelStep significantly improves the reliability of agents for tasks lasting hours or days, paving the way for more patient, proactive, and efficient AI assistants.
