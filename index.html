Crescent library brings privacy to digital identity systems

Digital identities offer convenience but create privacy risks through "linkability," where hidden identifiers can track user behavior across various digital interactions. Microsoft Research has released Crescent, a cryptographic library designed to add "unlinkability" to widely used digital identity formats like JSON Web Tokens and mobile driver's licenses, preventing such tracking. Crescent utilizes zero-knowledge SNARKs with an efficient two-stage process, allowing users to selectively disclose information and ensure their separate credential presentations remain unlinked without requiring updates from the organizations that issue these credentials.

Apple Announces Fourth Store in India

Apple is opening its fourth retail store in India, "Apple Koregaon Park," in Pune on Thursday, September 4 at 1:00 p.m. local time. This new location will launch shortly after a third store in Bengaluru, joining the two existing operational stores in Mumbai and New Delhi. Apple announced the Pune store with a "Namaskar, Pune" message and a distinctive peacock-feathered logo.
