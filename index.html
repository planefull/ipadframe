Tell me when: Building agents that can wait, monitor, and act

Modern LLM agents struggle with long-running monitoring tasks like tracking emails or price drops because they lack the ability to intelligently determine *when* to check, leading to inefficiency or failure. To address this, Microsoft Research introduces SentinelStep, a mechanism implemented in their Magentic-UI system that enables agents to patiently monitor conditions for extended periods by dynamically adjusting polling intervals and managing context. This innovation significantly improves agent reliability for tasks lasting hours, making them more proactive, efficient, and capable of handling real-world monitoring needs.

iOS 26.1 Coming Next Week: All the New Features

Apple is nearing the release of iOS 26.1, the first major update to its iOS 26 operating system, expected early next week after extensive beta testing. This update introduces a variety of new features, interface changes, and usability improvements across the system. Key additions include a Liquid Glass transparency toggle, an option to disable Lock Screen camera swipe, a new slide-to-stop feature for alarms, expanded language support for Apple Intelligence and AirPods Live Translation, and minor UI adjustments in several apps like Settings and Photos.
