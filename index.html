Ideas: More AI-resilient biosecurity with the Paraphrase Project

The "Paraphrase Project" revealed a critical "zero-day" vulnerability where AI tools could redesign toxic proteins, such as ricin, to bypass existing biosecurity screening systems used by DNA synthesis companies. A collaborative team developed an AI red-teaming pipeline to generate these "paraphrased" toxic sequences and subsequently created and implemented "patches" to strengthen these screening tools. This effort resulted in a more robust, AI-resilient biosecurity framework and established a novel "perpetual gatekeeping" approach for responsible data sharing in dual-use research, as detailed in their *Science* publication.
