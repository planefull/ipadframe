Crescent library brings privacy to digital identity systems

Digital identities, while convenient, introduce significant privacy risks through "linkability," allowing various uses of a credential to be connected and user profiles built. To counter this, Microsoft Research has developed Crescent, a cryptographic library that adds "unlinkability" to common digital identity formats like JSON Web Tokens and mobile driver's licenses. Crescent leverages zero-knowledge proofs and a two-stage process to enable users to selectively disclose information and prove facts about their credentials without revealing unnecessary details or linking separate interactions, integrating with existing systems without requiring infrastructure changes.

Apple Slashes 2025 MLS Season Pass to as Low as $25

Apple has significantly discounted the MLS Season Pass for the remainder of the 2025 season, offering it for $29, or $25 for Apple TV+ subscribers, a substantial drop from its usual $99 price. This reduced annual subscription includes access to all live and on-demand MLS matches, playoffs, "MLS 360," and "Sunday Night Soccer." Users should note that the pass will automatically renew at the full $99 price for the 2026 season unless manually canceled beforehand.
