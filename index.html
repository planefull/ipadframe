Breaking the networking wall in AI infrastructure 

Microsoft has developed MOSAIC, a novel optical link technology designed to overcome networking bottlenecks that limit AI system performance. This technology addresses the fundamental trade-off between power efficiency, reliability, and reach in current copper and optical data center links. By utilizing a "wide-and-slow" architecture with hundreds of parallel low-speed microLED channels, MOSAIC offers a solution with low power consumption, high reliability, and a long reach (up to 50 meters), paving the way for more scalable and efficient AI infrastructure designs.
