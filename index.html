When industry knowledge meets PIKE-RAG: The innovation behind Signify’s customer service boost

Signify, a leading LED lighting company, faced significant challenges in providing accurate and efficient answers to complex professional inquiries due to its vast and diverse technical documentation. To overcome this, Signify collaborated with Microsoft Research Asia to implement PIKE-RAG technology within its Azure-based knowledge management system. This integration led to a 12% improvement in answer accuracy, achieved through PIKE-RAG's ability to parse multimodal documents, establish an end-to-end knowledge loop, and perform dynamic multi-hop reasoning, demonstrating its strong potential for generalization across various industries.

tvOS 26.2 Lets You Create Apple TV Profiles Without an Account, Adds Dedicated Kids Mode

tvOS 26.2 introduces significant changes to Apple TV profiles, allowing users to create them without an Apple Account, which is ideal for guests or children. This update includes a dedicated "Create Profile" option that only requires a name and indicates if the user is a child. For child profiles, it provides a full kids-only view within the Apple TV app, automatically filtering content to age-appropriate ratings and offering granular control over movie and TV show access.
