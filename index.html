Ideas: More AI-resilient biosecurity with the Paraphrase Project

The Microsoft Research Podcast episode "Ideas: More AI-resilient biosecurity with the Paraphrase Project" discusses how AI's dual-use capabilities in protein design pose new biosecurity challenges. The Paraphrase Project uncovered that open-source AI tools could redesign toxic protein sequences, like ricin, to bypass existing screening systems, identifying a "zero day" vulnerability. In response, a cross-sector team developed and deployed more robust AI-resilient screening solutions and pioneered a "perpetual gatekeeping" strategy for sensitive data, balancing scientific transparency with mitigating potential catastrophic risks from biological misuse.
