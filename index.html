iPhone Driver's License Feature in Apple Wallet App Now Live in Illinois

Apple Wallet now allows residents in select U.S. states to add their driver's license or state ID to their iPhone and Apple Watch, offering a convenient and contactless method for displaying proof of identity or age. This feature recently launched in Illinois, expanding its availability to 14 U.S. states and Puerto Rico, with more states anticipated to join. Digital IDs in Apple Wallet are accepted at TSA checkpoints in over 250 airports and can also be used for age or identity verification at select businesses and within certain apps.
