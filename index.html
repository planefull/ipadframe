Applicability vs. job displacement: further notes on our recent research on AI and occupations

Microsoft Research clarified their recent paper on generative AI's occupational implications, emphasizing it aims to identify where AI chatbots are useful as a tool, not to predict job elimination. The study found AI most beneficial for knowledge work and communication tasks like writing and gathering information, where it can assist with subtasks. The authors caution against over-interpreting findings, as the methodology only evaluated chatbot applicability and does not capture the full complexity of real-world jobs.
