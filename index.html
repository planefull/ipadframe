Crescent library brings privacy to digital identity systems

Digital identities offer convenience but pose privacy risks through "linkability," where hidden identifiers enable tracking and profiling across various credential uses. To address this, Microsoft Research developed Crescent, a cryptographic library that adds "unlinkability" to popular digital identity formats like JSON Web Tokens and mobile driver's licenses. Utilizing efficient zero-knowledge proofs, Crescent allows users to selectively disclose information while preventing any connection between their separate digital interactions, all without requiring updates to existing issuing systems.
