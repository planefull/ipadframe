Apple Sees Record October in China With Strong iPhone 17 Demand

Apple experienced a record-breaking October in China, with iPhone sales soaring 37% year-over-year, primarily fueled by robust demand for the new iPhone 17 series. One in four smartphones sold in the country that month was an iPhone, marking a market share milestone not achieved since 2022, with the iPhone 17, 17 Pro, and 17 Pro Max models showing significant growth. This exceptional performance represents Apple's best-ever start to a December quarter, indicating strong momentum in the Chinese market despite upcoming competitor launches.
