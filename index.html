Using AI to assist in rare disease diagnosis

Microsoft Research, in collaboration with Drexel University and the Broad Institute, conducted a study to address the challenges genetic professionals face in diagnosing rare diseases through whole genome sequencing. They identified critical issues like information overload, inefficient collaboration, and difficulty prioritizing case reanalysis. The study proposes a generative AI assistant designed to flag cases for reanalysis based on new scientific findings and efficiently synthesize vast amounts of gene and variant data, aiming to streamline diagnosis and improve patient outcomes.
