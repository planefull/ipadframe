Tell me when: Building agents that can wait, monitor, and act

Modern LLM agents struggle with long-running monitoring tasks because they lack the ability to wait intelligently, either giving up too soon or wasting resources through constant checking. To address this, Microsoft Research has developed SentinelStep, a mechanism that enables agents to complete such tasks by wrapping them in a workflow with dynamic polling intervals and careful context management. This approach significantly improves agent reliability for extended monitoring, laying the groundwork for more practical, proactive, and resource-efficient AI assistants.

Apple CEO Tim Cook Says Revamped Siri on Track to Launch Next Year

Apple CEO Tim Cook confirmed that a more personalized Siri is on track to launch next year. These revamped features, which include improved personal context understanding and deeper per-app controls, are expected to debut on the iPhone as part of iOS 26.4 in March. This update follows previous delays and comes amidst class-action lawsuits over the postponed features.
