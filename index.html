Here Are All the States That Support Digital Driver's Licenses in the iPhone Wallet App

Currently, 12 U.S. states allow residents to store their driver's license or state ID in the Apple Wallet app, with several more states planning to add support. Additionally, all U.S. iPhone users can add their passport as a digital ID to Wallet. These digital IDs are accepted at most major TSA checkpoints for domestic travel and can be used for age verification, though a physical ID may still be required in some situations and specific device and OS versions are necessary.
