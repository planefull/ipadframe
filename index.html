Apple Sees Record October in China With Strong iPhone 17 Demand

Apple experienced a record-breaking October in China, with iPhone sales soaring 37% year-over-year thanks to robust demand for the new iPhone 17 models. This surge allowed Apple to capture a quarter of the Chinese smartphone market, a milestone not achieved since 2022, with the standard iPhone 17 leading growth among the new lineup. This strong performance, surpassing previous peaks, contributed significantly to an 8% overall growth in China's smartphone market and gives Apple strong momentum despite upcoming competition.
