MMCTAgent: Enabling multimodal reasoning over large video and image collections

Modern multimodal AI models struggle with complex visual reasoning over long-form video and large image collections, due to limitations in temporal context and iterative analysis. To address this, Microsoft Research developed MMCTAgent (Multi-modal Critical Thinking Agent), an AutoGen-based system featuring a Planner-Critic architecture and specialized ImageAgent and VideoAgent components. This framework enables iterative, tool-based reasoning and self-evaluation, significantly enhancing accuracy and providing a scalable, extensible solution for complex visual question-answering across diverse media.

Apple and OpenAI Must Face xAI Lawsuit Over Siri Integration

A Texas judge denied Apple and OpenAI's request to dismiss a lawsuit filed by Elon Musk's xAI startup, meaning the case will proceed. xAI sued the companies in August 2025, accusing them of conspiring to dominate the AI market through Apple's exclusive integration of ChatGPT with Siri. xAI alleges this arrangement unfairly disadvantages competing generative AI chatbots like its own Grok, depriving them of market access and data from iPhone users.
