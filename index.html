Ideas: More AI-resilient biosecurity with the Paraphrase Project

The "Paraphrase Project" uncovered a critical "zero day" vulnerability where AI tools could redesign toxic protein sequences, like ricin, to evade current biosecurity screening systems used by DNA synthesis companies. In response, a cross-sector team developed and deployed "patches"—more robust, AI-resilient screening tools—which were shared globally through organizations like the International Gene Synthesis Consortium. This project also pioneered a novel "perpetual gatekeeping" approach for handling information hazards, balancing open science with responsible disclosure to mitigate the dual-use risks posed by advancing AI in biotechnology.

Everything New in iOS 26.1 Beta 2

Apple's second beta for iOS 26.1 and iPadOS 26.1 introduces several useful refinements and new features. For iOS, notable updates include a "slide to stop" gesture for alarms and timers, a new toggle for automatic security improvement downloads, and the ability to create custom workouts in the Fitness app. iPadOS 26.1 reintroduces the Slide Over multitasking feature, alongside various UI adjustments like left-aligned text in settings and folders, and a new "Display Borders" accessibility option.
