Tell me when: Building agents that can wait, monitor, and act

Modern LLM agents currently struggle with long-running monitoring tasks, like tracking emails or price changes, because they cannot intelligently wait and manage context over extended periods without giving up or overflowing their memory. To solve this, Microsoft Research introduces SentinelStep, a mechanism that wraps agents in a workflow with dynamic polling and careful context management, allowing them to monitor conditions for hours or days. Integrated into their Magentic-UI prototype and open-sourced, SentinelStep significantly improves agent reliability for longer tasks, paving the way for more patient and proactive AI assistants.

Spotify Launches Redesigned Apple TV App With Music Videos, Podcasts

Spotify has launched a redesigned Apple TV app, rebuilt from the ground up for a faster, smarter, and more visual experience. The update introduces a refreshed design for tvOS 26 and brings previously mobile-exclusive features like video podcasts and music videos to the platform, with music video availability expanding globally. Additionally, the app offers Connect remote control, lyrics, Spotify DJ recommendations, seamless playback, and queue management, and is available for immediate download from the App Store.
