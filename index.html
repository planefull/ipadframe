Tell me when: Building agents that can wait, monitor, and act

Modern LLM agents struggle with long-running monitoring tasks, failing to know *when* to check for updates, leading to inefficiency or abandonment. To address this, Microsoft Research developed SentinelStep, a mechanism that enables agents to monitor conditions for extended periods by dynamically adjusting polling intervals and managing context to prevent overflow. Integrated into Magentic-UI, SentinelStep significantly improves the reliability of agents on longer tasks, laying the groundwork for more practical, proactive, and resource-efficient always-on assistants.
