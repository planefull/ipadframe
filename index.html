Crescent library brings privacy to digital identity systems

Digital identities, while convenient, introduce privacy risks through "linkability," which allows separate uses of credentials to be connected to an individual, enabling tracking. To combat this, Microsoft Research developed Crescent, a cryptographic library that adds "unlinkability" to common digital identity formats like JSON Web Tokens and mobile driver's licenses. Crescent utilizes zero-knowledge proofs and a two-stage process to allow users to selectively disclose information without revealing linking identifiers, thereby protecting privacy without requiring changes to existing issuing systems.

Apple Slashes 2025 MLS Season Pass to as Low as $25

Apple has significantly discounted the MLS Season Pass for the remainder of the 2025 season, offering it for $29, or $25 for Apple TV+ subscribers, down from the usual $99 annual price. This reduced rate covers all remaining live and on-demand MLS matches, playoffs, and exclusive features like "MLS 360" and "Sunday Night Soccer." Subscribers should be aware that the discounted annual pass will automatically renew at the standard $99 price for the 2026 season unless manually canceled before then.
