Ideas: More AI-resilient biosecurity with the Paraphrase Project

The Paraphrase Project, discussed in this Microsoft Research podcast, revealed a "zero-day" vulnerability where AI tools could redesign toxic proteins, allowing them to evade existing biosecurity screening systems. A collaborative team, including Microsoft, Twist Bioscience, and IBBIS, worked to develop and implement more robust screening solutions, sharing these "patches" globally to strengthen defenses. This effort not only enhanced biosecurity against potential AI misuse but also established a new model for responsible data sharing in dual-use research through a "perpetual gatekeeping" strategy.
