Using AI to assist in rare disease diagnosis

Genetic professionals face significant challenges interpreting vast whole-genome sequencing data for rare disease diagnosis, leading to low initial diagnostic rates and complex reanalysis. To address this, Microsoft Research, Drexel University, and the Broad Institute studied integrating a generative AI assistant to streamline this intensive process. The proposed AI tool aims to enhance diagnostic efficiency and reduce diagnosis time by flagging unsolved cases for reanalysis and synthesizing complex gene and variant information from scientific literature.

Samsung Confirms Plan to Make Foldable Displays for Major American Company

Samsung Display president Lee Cheong has confirmed plans to mass-produce foldable OLED displays for a major North American company, widely believed to be Apple. This announcement solidifies long-standing rumors about Apple's first foldable iPhone, with expectations of a launch in less than a year. The device is rumored to feature an inward-folding, crease-free display, Touch ID, and the A20 chip, potentially arriving alongside the iPhone 18 lineup in 2026.
