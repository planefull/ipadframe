Everything New in iOS 26.2 Beta 2

Apple has released the second beta of iOS 26.2 to developers, introducing several new features and design updates. Notable changes include a "Liquid Glass" design for the Measure app's level, an option to sort games by size in the Games app, and support for disabling pinned messages in CarPlay. The beta also offers more information on enhanced safety alerts for emergencies and features quicker, bouncier menu animations across the system.
