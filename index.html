MMCTAgent: Enabling multimodal reasoning over large video and image collections

Modern multimodal AI models struggle with complex real-world reasoning over long-form videos and vast image libraries, due to limitations in temporal context and iterative analysis. To address this, Microsoft Research developed MMCTAgent, a Multi-modal Critical Thinking Agent built on AutoGen with a Planner-Critic architecture. This system enables structured, iterative reasoning, leveraging modality-specific agents and tools to enhance accuracy, explainability, and scalability for complex visual question-answering across diverse multimodal data.
