RenderFormer: How neural networks are reshaping 3D rendering

Microsoft Research has developed RenderFormer, a novel neural architecture that entirely replaces traditional physics-based 3D rendering techniques with machine learning. This model is the first to learn a complete graphics rendering pipeline, supporting arbitrary 3D scenes and global illumination without relying on ray tracing or rasterization. RenderFormer represents a significant step forward for neural rendering, offering a robust foundation for future integration with broader AI systems and opening new possibilities for visual computing.
