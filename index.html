When industry knowledge meets PIKE-RAG: The innovation behind Signify’s customer service boost

Signify collaborated with Microsoft Research Asia to integrate PIKE-RAG technology into its knowledge management system, addressing challenges in accurately answering complex technical inquiries about its vast product range. PIKE-RAG improved answer accuracy by 12% by processing multimodal content like charts and tables, establishing citation relationships from original documents, and performing multi-hop reasoning for intricate questions. This innovation enhanced Signify's customer service and demonstrated PIKE-RAG's strong generalization capabilities for professional knowledge Q&A across various industries.

Apple Store With All-New Design Opening on Long Island This Month

Apple's Roosevelt Field store in Garden City, New York, is set to reopen on Friday, November 21, following extensive renovations that will introduce a modern design with more wood paneling. The store, originally opened in 2002 at Long Island's largest shopping mall, has been closed for approximately a year, with a temporary location serving customers during the revamp. This renovation is part of a larger strategy by Apple, as the company is also updating multiple other U.S. stores, recently opened a relocated store in Texas, and completed renovations at an Australian location.
