Tell me when: Building agents that can wait, monitor, and act

Large Language Model (LLM) agents struggle with long-running monitoring tasks, such as tracking emails or price changes, because they lack the ability to efficiently manage polling frequency and context over extended periods. To address this, Microsoft Research introduces SentinelStep, a new mechanism within their Magentic-UI agentic system. SentinelStep enables agents to perform these tasks effectively by dynamically adjusting polling intervals and carefully managing context to prevent overflow, significantly improving reliability for tasks lasting hours or days.
