Apple's Extended Return Policy Now in Effect for 2025 Holiday Season

Apple has activated its extended return policy for the 2025 holiday shopping season, covering most products purchased online or in stores. For many countries, items bought between November 12 and December 25 can be returned until January 8, 2026, with some regions extending the window to January 20, 2026, though carrier-financed iPhones are typically excluded. Additionally, Apple's online store now features holiday gift recommendations, including older products like the HomePod mini and Apple TV, despite existing inventory concerns and past update rumors.
