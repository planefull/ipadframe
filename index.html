Tell me when: Building agents that can wait, monitor, and act

Modern LLM agents struggle with long-running monitoring tasks like watching for price drops or email responses because they don't know *when* to check, leading to wasted resources or early failure. To address this, Microsoft Research developed SentinelStep, a mechanism that enables agents to perform such tasks by wrapping them in a workflow with dynamic polling intervals and careful context management. This approach significantly improves agent reliability for tasks lasting hours or days, fostering the development of more patient, efficient, and proactive AI assistants.

Adobe Photoshop Adds AI Assistant to Automate Repetitive Design Tasks

Adobe has announced a new AI Assistant for Photoshop and Adobe Express, aimed at automating repetitive design tasks, offering personalized recommendations, and enabling conversational creation and editing. Photoshop also receives several other AI enhancements, including Generative Upscale, an expanded Generative Fill with partner models like Gemini 2.5 Flash, and a Harmonize feature for blending objects into new scenes. While Generative Upscale, Generative Fill, and Harmonize are available now, the Photoshop AI Assistant is accessible via a private beta waitlist, and the Adobe Express AI Assistant is for Premium customers.
