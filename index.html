Apple Questions Funding Behind UK iCloud Lawsuit

Apple has challenged the funding behind a $4 billion UK class-action lawsuit alleging its iCloud storage practices unfairly lock in users and lead to higher costs. The tech giant raised concerns about the financial stability of the third-party funder, Litigation Capital Management (LCM), which recently suffered a severe share value decline, questioning its ability to cover Apple's legal costs if the lawsuit fails. This dispute unfolds as Apple faces increased antitrust scrutiny, and the tribunal's decision on the funding is pending.
