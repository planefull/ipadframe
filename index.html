Tell me when: Building agents that can wait, monitor, and act

Modern LLM agents excel at complex tasks but fail at simple monitoring due to their inability to know *when* to check for updates, leading to wasted resources or task abandonment. To solve this, Microsoft Research introduces SentinelStep, a mechanism that wraps agents in a workflow with dynamic polling and careful context management. This innovation allows agents to reliably perform long-running monitoring tasks for hours or days, significantly improving their success rates and paving the way for more practical and proactive AI assistants.

iOS 26.1 Beta Liquid Glass Battery Drain Test: Tinted vs Clear Mode

The article details a battery life test for Apple's new "Liquid Glass" feature in iOS 26.1, investigating whether the "Tinted" option or accessibility settings like "Reduce Transparency" impact battery drain. The author conducted identical 2.5-hour app usage tests on an iPhone 17 Pro Max with various Liquid Glass configurations. The results showed no meaningful difference in battery consumption across any of the settings, concluding that Liquid Glass-related options do not significantly affect battery life on newer iPhones.
