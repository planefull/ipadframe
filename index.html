Apple Releases Safari Technology Preview 232 With Bug Fixes and Performance Improvements

Apple has released update 232 for Safari Technology Preview, its experimental browser designed for testing features planned for future Safari versions. This update includes various fixes and improvements for CSS, JavaScript, Media, and more, and is compatible with macOS Sequoia and Tahoe. Safari Technology Preview, which runs alongside the regular Safari browser, is available through Software Update without needing a developer account, allowing Apple to gather feedback on its development process.
