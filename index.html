MMCTAgent: Enabling multimodal reasoning over large video and image collections

Modern multimodal AI models struggle with complex reasoning over long-form videos and large image collections due to limitations in context and iterative processing. To address this, Microsoft Research developed MMCTAgent, a Multi-modal Critical Thinking Agent built on AutoGen with a Planner-Critic architecture. This system employs modality-specific agents and an iterative reasoning loop with specialized tools to enable explainable, accurate, and scalable analysis of vast visual data.

Red Dead Redemption and Undead Nightmare Coming to iOS on Dec 2
