Using AI to assist in rare disease diagnosis

Genetic professionals face significant challenges in diagnosing rare diseases due to information overload, complex data synthesis from whole genome sequencing, and difficulties prioritizing reanalysis of unsolved cases. To address this, Microsoft Research, Drexel University, and the Broad Institute co-designed a generative AI assistant to enhance sensemaking in genetic analysis. This AI aims to streamline workflows by flagging cases for reanalysis based on new scientific findings and synthesizing vast amounts of gene and variant information, ultimately improving diagnostic yield and patient outcomes.

10+ macOS Tahoe Features You Might Have Missed

macOS Tahoe introduces many new features beyond its visible design changes, focusing on enhancing user experience and productivity. Notable additions include expanded customization for the menu bar and lock screen, improved Spotlight functionalities like clipboard history, Quick Keys, and refined search, alongside iOS-style automations and cross-device Live Activities. The update also brings gaming optimizations such as a dedicated Low Power Mode and a new Game Overlay, making the Mac more versatile and personalized.
