iPhone 17 vs. iPhone 16 Wi-Fi Speeds: New Study Reveals the Winner

A new Ookla study found that the iPhone 17, iPhone 17 Pro, iPhone 17 Pro Max, and iPhone Air models offer significantly faster and more consistent Wi-Fi speeds than the iPhone 16 series, thanks to Apple's custom-designed N1 chip. This N1 chip provides up to 40% faster average download and upload speeds, improving performance across various countries and challenging Wi-Fi conditions. While the N1 chip's Wi-Fi 7 limitation proved negligible in real-world use, Google's Pixel 10 Pro series slightly surpassed the iPhone 17 globally in average Wi-Fi download speeds.
