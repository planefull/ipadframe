Tell me when: Building agents that can wait, monitor, and act

Modern LLM agents struggle with long-running monitoring tasks like tracking emails or price drops because they lack the ability to know *when* to check, leading to inefficient polling or context overflow. To address this, Microsoft Research developed SentinelStep, a mechanism that wraps agents in a workflow with dynamic polling and careful context management, enabling them to patiently monitor conditions for hours or days. Integrated into their Magentic-UI prototype, SentinelStep significantly improves task reliability over extended durations, laying the groundwork for more practical and proactive always-on AI assistants.
