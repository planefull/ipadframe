Using AI to assist in rare disease diagnosis

Genetic professionals face significant challenges, including information overload and complex data synthesis, when diagnosing rare diseases through whole genome sequencing, leading to low initial diagnosis rates. To address this, Microsoft Research, Drexel University, and the Broad Institute developed a generative AI assistant prototype designed to flag cases for reanalysis and aggregate scientific literature about genes and variants. This AI-enhanced system aims to streamline workflows, improve diagnostic yield, and ultimately reduce the time it takes to diagnose rare genetic conditions.

T-Mobile's Satellite Service Now Works With Popular Apps

T-Mobile is expanding its T-Satellite with Starlink service, allowing users to access popular apps via satellite data when Wi-Fi or cellular connections are unavailable. This new connectivity supports a range of applications, including Google Maps, WhatsApp, and AccuWeather, offering tailored, critical content for essential use. The service is compatible with iPhones, included in specific T-Mobile plans, and available for other subscribers or standalone users for $10 per month.
