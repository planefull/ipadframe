Using AI to assist in rare disease diagnosis

Genetic professionals face significant challenges in diagnosing rare genetic diseases due to the vast and complex data from whole genome sequencing, resulting in low initial diagnosis rates and long waits for patients. To address this, a study by Microsoft Research, Drexel University, and the Broad Institute explored designing a generative AI assistant that supports "sensemaking" tasks. This AI tool helps by flagging cases for reanalysis based on new scientific findings and synthesizing critical gene and variant information, aiming to improve diagnostic efficiency and ultimately enhance patient quality of life.

Amazon Unveils New Echo Speakers with Alexa+ AI and Omnisense Technology

Amazon recently unveiled new Echo Dot Max, Echo Studio, Echo Show 8, and Echo Show 11 smart speakers, all featuring the new Alexa+ AI assistant. These devices incorporate faster chips with AI acceleration and a custom Omnisense sensor platform, enabling ambient AI capabilities like event-based reminders and security alerts. The updated speakers offer improved audio and visual experiences, directly competing with Apple's HomePod, with an Alexa+ Store and integration with other brands planned for the future.
