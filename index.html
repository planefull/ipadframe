Another Designer Leaves Apple

Abidur Chowdhury, the designer who introduced the iPhone Air and was considered a rising star, has departed Apple to join an AI startup, a move that reportedly "made waves" internally. His departure is part of a broader trend of high-profile industrial designers leaving Apple since Jony Ive's exit in 2019, further depleting the ranks of those who worked under Ive. Following former operations chief Jeff Williams' recent retirement, Apple's design team will now report directly to CEO Tim Cook, marking a significant leadership shift.
