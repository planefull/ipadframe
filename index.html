Tell me when: Building agents that can wait, monitor, and act

Modern LLM agents struggle with long-running monitoring tasks, such as tracking emails or price changes, because they lack the ability to intelligently determine *when* to check, leading to inefficiency or failure. To address this, Microsoft Research introduces SentinelStep, a mechanism that enables agents to complete these tasks by wrapping them in a workflow with dynamic polling and careful context management. This approach significantly improves agent reliability for extended monitoring tasks, allowing them to remain proactive without wasting resources, and is now open-sourced as part of Magentic-UI.

iOS 26.1 Coming Next Week: All the New Features

Apple is nearing the release of iOS 26.1, the first major update to iOS 26, which is expected early next week and introduces useful new features and interface changes. Key additions include a toggle for Liquid Glass transparency, the ability to disable the Lock Screen camera swipe, a slide-to-stop function for alarms, and expanded language support for Apple Intelligence and AirPods Live Translation. The update also brings custom workout creation in the Fitness app, numerous UI alignment refinements, and for iPadOS 26.1, the reintroduction of Slide Over multitasking.
