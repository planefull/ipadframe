Apple and OpenAI Must Face xAI Lawsuit Over Siri Integration

A Texas District Judge has denied Apple and OpenAI's bid to dismiss a lawsuit filed by Elon Musk's xAI startup, meaning the case will proceed. xAI accuses Apple and OpenAI of conspiring to dominate the AI market, specifically by integrating ChatGPT exclusively into Siri and neglecting competing AI products like Grok. The lawsuit claims this anti-competitive scheme unfairly limits user choice and deprives xAI of critical data and market access.
