Tell me when: Building agents that can wait, monitor, and act

Modern LLM agents struggle with long-running monitoring tasks, such as waiting for emails or price drops, because they lack the ability to intelligently determine *when* to check, leading to resource waste or failure. To address this, Microsoft Research introduces SentinelStep, a mechanism integrated into their Magentic-UI prototype that allows agents to patiently monitor conditions for hours or days. SentinelStep achieves this by dynamically adjusting polling intervals and managing context, significantly improving agent reliability for extended tasks and enabling proactive, resource-efficient automation.

Apple Hosts Special Vision Pro Event for Developers

Apple recently hosted a two-day "Meet with Apple" program for developers, focusing on building immersive media and interactive apps for the M5 Vision Pro and visionOS 26. Developers attended sessions on creating Apple Immersive Video, designing spatial interactions, and utilizing features like SharePlay and spatial Personas. The program aimed to help developers tell impactful immersive stories and create new media experiences, with full event recordings available on Apple's Developer YouTube channel.
