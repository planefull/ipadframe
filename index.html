Tell me when: Building agents that can wait, monitor, and act

Modern LLM agents excel at complex tasks but fail at simple monitoring due to their inability to intelligently "wait" and check conditions over extended periods without wasting resources or overflowing context. To address this, Microsoft Research introduces SentinelStep, a mechanism that enables agents to perform long-running monitoring tasks by dynamically adjusting polling intervals and managing context. This innovation significantly improves agent reliability for tasks lasting hours or days, laying the groundwork for more proactive and efficient AI assistants.

iOS 26.1 Coming Next Week: All the New Features

Apple is finalizing beta testing for iOS 26.1 and iPadOS 26.1, with a public release anticipated early next week. This significant update introduces numerous interface refinements, such as a customizable Liquid Glass transparency toggle, left-aligned text in Settings and folders, and a "slide to stop" mechanism for alarms. Key new features also include the ability to disable Lock Screen camera swipe and phone call haptics, expanded language support for Apple Intelligence and AirPods Live Translation, and the reintroduction of Slide Over multitasking for iPads.
