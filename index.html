When industry knowledge meets PIKE-RAG: The innovation behind Signify’s customer service boost

Signify, a leader in LED lighting, struggled to efficiently provide accurate answers to professional users due to vast product data, complex parameters, and diverse technical documentation. To overcome this, they partnered with Microsoft Research Asia to implement PIKE-RAG technology into their Azure-based knowledge management system. This specialized RAG system, which handles multimodal documents, uses end-to-end knowledge loops, and performs multi-hop reasoning, significantly improved answer accuracy by 12% in a proof-of-concept, validating its effectiveness for complex industrial knowledge management.
