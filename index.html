Using AI to assist in rare disease diagnosis

Microsoft Research, in collaboration with Drexel University and the Broad Institute, conducted a study on integrating generative AI to assist genetic professionals in diagnosing rare genetic diseases through whole genome sequencing. The study identified challenges like information overload and difficulty prioritizing reanalysis, then co-designed an AI assistant to address these by synthesizing vast biomedical data and flagging cases for re-evaluation. This AI-driven approach aims to enhance diagnostic efficiency, reduce diagnosis time, and ultimately improve outcomes for patients with unsolved rare genetic conditions.
