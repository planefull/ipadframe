Using AI to assist in rare disease diagnosis

Diagnosing rare genetic diseases is challenging due to the massive, complex data from whole genome sequencing, leading to low initial diagnosis rates and time-consuming reanalysis for genetic professionals. Microsoft Research, in collaboration with Drexel University and the Broad Institute, developed a generative AI-based assistant to address these obstacles by streamlining data analysis. This AI tool aims to improve diagnosis rates and patient outcomes by synthesizing biomedical data, flagging cases for reanalysis based on new findings, and aggregating critical gene and variant information.

Everything New in iOS 26.1 Beta 1

Apple has released the first beta of iOS 26.1, just a week after iOS 26, with its main focus being the addition of new languages to Apple Intelligence and AirPods Live Translation. This update introduces support for languages like Danish, Dutch, Japanese, Korean, and various Chinese dialects in these features. Other notable improvements include a Liquid Glass keypad in the Phone app, swipe gestures for switching songs in Apple Music, updated video scrubbing in Photos, full-color calendar event backgrounds, a wider Safari tab bar, and the renaming of Rapid Security Updates to Background Security Improvement.
