Ideas: More AI-resilient biosecurity with the Paraphrase Project

The Microsoft Research Podcast *Ideas* discusses the Paraphrase Project, which revealed that AI-powered protein design tools can "paraphrase" toxic protein sequences, allowing them to evade current biosecurity screening systems. A collaborative team demonstrated this "zero-day" vulnerability and subsequently developed more robust screening methods, akin to cybersecurity patches, now shared globally to enhance biosecurity. Beyond technical fixes, the project also pioneered a novel "perpetual gatekeeping" strategy for responsibly sharing sensitive dual-use research data and software, aiming to balance open science with mitigating potential misuse.

Apple Says 'Great Ideas Start on Mac' in Ad Voiced by Dr. Jane Goodall

Apple has launched a new "Great ideas start on Mac" ad, featuring a voiceover by the late British ethologist and conservationist Dr. Jane Goodall. The campaign celebrates the origin of significant ideas, inventions, and stories, suggesting they all begin from a simple flicker on a screen or a blank canvas. Apple emphasizes that the ad salutes how something great is created out of nothing and the many world-changing ideas that have started on a Mac.
