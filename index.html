Ideas: More AI-resilient biosecurity with the Paraphrase Project

The Microsoft Research Podcast episode "Ideas: More AI-resilient biosecurity with the Paraphrase Project" explores how AI-powered protein design poses a "dual-use" dilemma, enabling both scientific breakthroughs and potential biosecurity threats. The Paraphrase Project, led by Eric Horvitz and a cross-sector team, demonstrated that AI could redesign toxic proteins to evade existing screening systems, identifying a critical vulnerability. In response, the team developed more robust screening tools and a novel "perpetual gatekeeping" strategy for responsible data sharing, aiming to strengthen global biosecurity while balancing transparency and mitigating information hazards.
