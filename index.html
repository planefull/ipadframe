Tell me when: Building agents that can wait, monitor, and act

Modern LLM agents struggle with long-running monitoring tasks like watching for price drops or email responses because they lack the ability to efficiently manage polling frequency and context over extended periods. To address this, Microsoft Research introduces SentinelStep, a mechanism integrated into Magentic-UI that enables agents to dynamically adjust polling intervals and manage context overflow. This innovation significantly improves agent reliability for tasks lasting hours or days, laying the groundwork for more patient, proactive, and resource-efficient AI assistants.
