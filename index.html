Apple's 2026 Mac Plans

Apple has extensive Mac updates planned for 2026, bringing M5 chips to most models across the MacBook Air, MacBook Pro, Mac mini, and Mac Studio lines. The year will also see the introduction of a new low-cost MacBook with an A18 Pro chip, refreshed Mac displays, and a potential M5 Ultra for the Mac Studio. Notably, a revamped OLED MacBook Pro featuring a touchscreen and next-generation M6 chip is also a possibility toward the end of 2026.
