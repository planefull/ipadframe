Ideas: More AI-resilient biosecurity with the Paraphrase Project

The Microsoft Research Podcast *Ideas* discusses the Paraphrase Project, which investigated how AI-powered protein design tools present a "dual-use" challenge for biosecurity. Researchers discovered that AI could redesign toxic proteins, such as ricin, in ways that evaded existing biosecurity screening systems designed to prevent their creation. In response, a cross-sector team developed a cybersecurity-style patch, creating more robust screening solutions and pioneering a new perpetual gatekeeping strategy for responsibly sharing sensitive research details to enhance global biosecurity against emerging AI threats.
