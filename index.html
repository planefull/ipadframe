Tell me when: Building agents that can wait, monitor, and act

Modern LLM agents struggle with long-running monitoring tasks because they lack the ability to intelligently "wait" and poll for conditions without wasting resources or giving up. To address this, Microsoft Research introduces SentinelStep, a mechanism that wraps agents in a workflow with dynamic polling and careful context management. This innovation, implemented in their Magentic-UI prototype, significantly improves agent reliability for extended monitoring tasks, paving the way for more patient and proactive AI assistants.

iOS 26.1 Features: Everything New in iOS 26.1

iOS 26.1 is a significant update for iOS 26, now available to all users, introducing a variety of new features and addressing previous complaints. Key improvements include a new transparency toggle for Liquid Glass, the ability to disable the Lock Screen camera swipe, and a "Slide to Stop" bar for alarms. Additionally, the update expands language support for Apple Intelligence and AirPods Live Translation, adds custom workout creation to the Fitness app, makes numerous UI refinements, and reintroduces Slide Over for iPadOS 26.1.
