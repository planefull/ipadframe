Ideas: More AI-resilient biosecurity with the Paraphrase Project

The Paraphrase Project, led by Microsoft Chief Scientific Officer Eric Horvitz and a cross-sector team, investigated the dual-use nature of AI in protein design, demonstrating that open-source AI tools could redesign toxic proteins to bypass existing biosecurity screening systems. This discovery, deemed a "zero-day" vulnerability, prompted a cybersecurity-style response, leading to the development of more robust protein screening mechanisms. The project successfully strengthened global biosecurity against AI-enabled threats and established a novel, perpetually gatekept peer-review process for responsibly sharing sensitive research.

Apple Seeds Third Public Betas of iOS 26.1, iPadOS 26.1 and macOS Tahoe 26.1

Apple has released the third public betas for iOS 26.1, iPadOS 26.1, macOS Tahoe 26.1, tvOS 26.1, and watchOS 26.1, which public testers can download via the Software Update section. These updates primarily expand Apple Intelligence to several new languages, including Danish, Dutch, and Vietnamese, and introduce AirPods Live Translation for Japanese, Korean, Italian, and Chinese. Additionally, the betas include various minor changes to apps like Photos, Music, Apple TV, and Phone.
