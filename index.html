When industry knowledge meets PIKE-RAG: The innovation behind Signify’s customer service boost

Signify, a global leader in LED lighting, faced significant challenges in its knowledge management system, struggling to efficiently provide accurate answers to professional users due to vast product data and complex technical documentation. To address this, Signify collaborated with Microsoft Research Asia to implement PIKE-RAG technology, integrating it into their Microsoft Azure-based system. This advanced RAG solution, which processes multimodal documents and performs multi-hop reasoning, improved answer accuracy by 12% and demonstrated strong generalization capabilities for complex industrial knowledge across various sectors.

Samsung SmartThings for iPhone Gets Siri Shortcuts Support and Enhanced Apple Watch Features

Samsung has updated its SmartThings app for iPhone, introducing several new features beneficial for households with both Galaxy and Apple devices. Key improvements include enhanced Apple Watch integration, allowing users to view and control device groups and routines directly from their wrist. Additionally, the app now supports Siri Shortcut controls for SmartThings routines, enabling voice commands to trigger automations, alongside expanded Live Activities showing up to five recently used devices.
