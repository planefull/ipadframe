Ideas: More AI-resilient biosecurity with the Paraphrase Project

The Paraphrase Project, detailed in a Microsoft Research podcast, addresses the "dual-use" dilemma of AI in protein design, where generative models can reformulate toxic proteins to bypass existing biosecurity screening systems. Researchers uncovered this "zero day" vulnerability, demonstrating that AI-designed toxic protein variants could evade detection by major DNA synthesis companies. In response, a cross-sector team developed and deployed "patches"—a more robust screening system and a novel "perpetual gatekeeping" approach for responsible data sharing—to enhance global biosecurity and set a precedent for managing information hazards in dual-use AI research.

Apple Watch's High Blood Pressure Notifications Available in Canada Starting Today

Apple's Hypertension Notifications feature is now available in Canada for select Apple Watch models (Series 9, 10, 11, Ultra 2, and 3). This feature passively analyzes optical heart sensor data over 30-day periods to detect consistent signs of chronic high blood pressure, notifying users without directly measuring blood pressure. It is not intended for individuals under 22, those already diagnosed with hypertension, or during pregnancy.
