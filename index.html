Tell me when: Building agents that can wait, monitor, and act

Modern LLM agents struggle with long-running monitoring tasks, such as tracking emails or price drops, because they lack the ability to intelligently determine *when* to check, leading to resource waste or task failure. To address this, Microsoft Research developed SentinelStep, a mechanism implemented in their Magentic-UI agentic system, which enables agents to patiently monitor conditions for extended periods. SentinelStep achieves this by dynamically adjusting polling intervals based on task context and managing agent state to prevent context overflow, demonstrating significant improvements in reliability for tasks lasting hours or days.

iOS 26.1 Coming Next Week: All the New Features

Apple is finalizing beta testing for iOS 26.1, the first major update to iOS 26, with an expected release early next week. This update brings several new features and interface changes, such as a toggle for Liquid Glass transparency, the ability to disable Lock Screen camera swipe, slide-to-stop for alarms, and custom workout creation in the Fitness app. Additionally, it expands language support for Apple Intelligence and AirPods Live Translation, and reintroduces Slide Over multitasking for iPadOS 26.1.
