When industry knowledge meets PIKE-RAG: The innovation behind Signify’s customer service boost

Signify, a leading LED lighting company, faced challenges providing accurate and efficient answers to professional users due to complex product data and diverse documentation. To address this, they partnered with Microsoft Research Asia, integrating PIKE-RAG technology into their knowledge management system built on Microsoft Azure. This advanced solution, which handles multimodal content and performs multi-hop reasoning, achieved a 12% improvement in answer accuracy, demonstrating its potential for robust, industry-specific knowledge management.

Apple Announces Next Swift Student Challenge Ahead of WWDC 2026

Apple has announced that submissions for the 2026 Swift Student Challenge will be accepted from February 6 through February 28, 2026. This annual competition invites eligible student developers worldwide to create interactive "app playgrounds" using Swift Playground or Xcode to showcase their coding abilities. Winners will be recognized for innovation, creativity, social impact, or inclusivity, with "Distinguished Winners" receiving a three-day visit to Apple in Cupertino, potentially aligning with WWDC 2026.
