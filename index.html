iPhone Driver's License Feature Launching in One of Biggest U.S. States

Apple Wallet allows residents in select U.S. states to add their driver's license or state ID to their iPhone and Apple Watch for convenient, contactless proof of identity or age. This feature will launch in Illinois starting November 19, 2025, supported at Chicago's O'Hare and Midway, and St. Louis' Lambert airports, alongside a new Mobile ID Verifier app for businesses. Currently, this digital ID capability is available in 13 states and Puerto Rico, requiring compatible iPhone or Apple Watch models running recent operating systems.
