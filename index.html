Another Designer Leaves Apple

Abidur Chowdhury, a rising star designer known for introducing the iPhone Air, has left Apple to join an AI startup, reportedly making waves internally. His departure is unrelated to the iPhone Air's sales and adds to a trend of high-profile designers leaving Apple since Jony Ive's exit in 2019. Consequently, with former operations chief Jeff Williams' recent retirement, Apple's design team will now report directly to CEO Tim Cook.
