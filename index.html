Apple Announces Launch of U.S. Passport Feature in iPhone's Wallet App

Apple has introduced a new Digital ID feature in the Wallet app, enabling iPhone and Apple Watch users to create a digital version of their U.S. passport for convenient identity verification. Users can set up their Digital ID by scanning their physical passport, reading its embedded chip, and completing biometric verification steps on their device. This digital credential is currently accepted at over 250 U.S. airport TSA checkpoints for domestic travel, though it cannot replace a physical passport for international travel.
