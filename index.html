Tell me when: Building agents that can wait, monitor, and act

Modern LLM agents struggle with long-running monitoring tasks because they lack the ability to intelligently wait and know *when* to check for updates, leading to resource waste or task failure. To address this, Microsoft Research developed SentinelStep, a mechanism that enables agents to complete such tasks by wrapping them in a workflow with dynamic polling and careful context management. Implemented in their Magentic-UI prototype and open-sourced, SentinelStep significantly improves agent reliability for extended monitoring, laying the groundwork for more proactive and efficient AI assistants.
