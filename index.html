Tell me when: Building agents that can wait, monitor, and act

Modern LLM agents struggle with long-running monitoring tasks because they lack the ability to wait and poll at appropriate intervals, leading to inefficiency or failure. To address this, Microsoft Research introduced SentinelStep, a mechanism within their Magentic-UI system that enables agents to perform patient monitoring tasks. SentinelStep dynamically adjusts polling frequency and manages context to prevent overflow, significantly improving agent reliability and efficiency for tasks lasting hours.
