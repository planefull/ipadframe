Apple Store App Gets Liquid Glass Update With New Icon

Apple has updated its Apple Store app for iPhone and iPad, introducing a new icon and incorporating Liquid Glass design elements to match the aesthetic of iOS 26 and iPadOS 26. The new icon features a Liquid Glass shopping bag on a blue gradient background, a departure from the prior white background. These visual changes extend to a transparent navigation bar with a Liquid Glass slider and an updated search interface, though the app's core content remains unchanged.
