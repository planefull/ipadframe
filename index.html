Ideas: More AI-resilient biosecurity with the Paraphrase Project

The Microsoft Research "Paraphrase Project" uncovered a critical vulnerability where AI-powered protein design tools could reformulate toxic proteins, enabling them to bypass current biosecurity screening systems, dubbed the first "zero day" in AI and biosecurity. In response, a cross-sector team developed AI biosecurity red-teaming approaches, identifying weaknesses and creating "patches" to strengthen protein screening systems. This collaborative effort not only improved global biosecurity measures but also pioneered a new, perpetually gatekept peer review process for handling sensitive scientific information, setting a precedent for addressing dual-use dilemmas in emerging technologies.

Everything New in iOS 26.1 Beta 2

Apple has released the second beta of iOS 26.1 and iPadOS 26.1, introducing a range of useful changes and design refinements. Key updates include a "slide to stop" gesture for alarms and timers, a new toggle for automatic security improvements, and the ability to create custom workouts in the Fitness app. For iPadOS, Slide Over multitasking has been reintroduced, while other changes encompass UI alignment tweaks in Settings and Home Screen folders, and a more subtle Liquid Glass effect.
