Ideas: More AI-resilient biosecurity with the Paraphrase Project

The article discusses the "Paraphrase Project," a collaborative effort that exposed a critical vulnerability in biosecurity: AI tools could be used to redesign toxic proteins, like ricin, in ways that evaded existing screening systems. This "zero-day" threat prompted a cybersecurity-style response, leading to the development and implementation of more robust screening tools for nucleic acid synthesis companies. The project also pioneered a novel "perpetual gatekeeping" approach for managing sensitive research data, aiming to balance open science with minimizing information hazards for future dual-use technology challenges.

iOS 26 Liquid Glass Design Copied by Android Smartphone Maker

Chinese smartphone maker Vivo's new OriginOS 6 operating system update heavily draws inspiration from Apple's Liquid Glass design language, as seen in the unreleased iOS 26. The new OS features strikingly similar elements, including rounded buttons, a translucent glass look, squircle icons, and wallpapers with 2D to 3D effects. Despite Vivo's marketing referencing water, the interface's design makes it easily mistakable for iOS 26.
