Tool-space interference in the MCP era: Designing for agent compatibility at scale

The article highlights "tool-space interference" as a significant challenge emerging from the proliferation of AI agents and tools leveraging the Model Context Protocol (MCP). This interference arises from issues like large tool counts, complex parameter schemas, ambiguous naming, and poor error handling across diverse tools, reducing end-to-end effectiveness and hindering cooperation. To address these growing pains, the authors propose recommendations for protocol developers (e.g., formal namespaces), server developers (transparent documentation), client developers (optimizations and conflict resolution), and market developers (centralized compatibility and optimization services).

Apple Store Near Sydney Permanently Closing Next Month

Apple will permanently close its Westfield Hornsby store in Hornsby, Australia, on October 4. This decision stems from Apple's choice not to renew its lease, coinciding with the planned expansion and reopening of its nearby Chatswood Chase store in October. All employees from the Hornsby location will be offered transfers to the renovated Chatswood Chase store.
