Tool-space interference in the MCP era: Designing for agent compatibility at scale

The rise of agentic AI and the Model Context Protocol (MCP) enables widespread horizontal integration of diverse tools, but this introduces "tool-space interference" that reduces overall effectiveness. Key challenges include overwhelming tool catalogs, excessively long tool responses, complex parameter schemas, tool name collisions, and inadequate error messages, all of which hinder agent performance. To mitigate these issues, the article recommends implementing formal namespaces, improving resource sharing conventions, publishing transparent server documentation, and developing client-side optimizations to foster a more reliable and scalable multi-agent ecosystem.

Apple Reintroduces mmWave 5G Antenna Window on iPhone 17 Pro Models in U.S.

The iPhone 17 Pro and Pro Max models in the U.S. will reintroduce an antenna window on their top edge to allow mmWave 5G signals to pass through. This feature was previously present on iPhone 12-15 but removed for the iPhone 16, and is now necessary due to the new aluminum unibody design which obstructs radio signals. The antenna window and mmWave 5G support will not be available on standard iPhone 17 models, iPhone Air, or any iPhones sold outside the U.S., as mmWave is a fast, short-range 5G technology ideal for dense urban areas.
