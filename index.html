Everything New in iOS 26.2 Beta 2

Apple has released the second beta of iOS 26.2 for developers, introducing several new features and design enhancements. Key updates include a "Liquid Glass" design for the Measure app's level, new sorting options by size in the Games app, CarPlay support for disabling pinned messages, and bouncier menu animations. The beta also provides more details on Enhanced Safety Alerts, which notify users of emergency events like earthquakes or floods and are enabled by default.
