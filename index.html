Ideas: More AI-resilient biosecurity with the Paraphrase Project

The Paraphrase Project uncovered a "zero-day" vulnerability where AI-powered protein design tools could reformulate toxic proteins to evade existing biosecurity screening systems, posing a significant risk. A collaborative team of experts responded by developing an AI biosecurity "red-teaming" pipeline to identify such weaknesses and create robust screening "patches" now shared globally with DNA synthesis companies. This effort not only strengthened biosecurity defenses but also pioneered a "perpetual gatekeeping" approach for responsible sharing of sensitive scientific data, offering a model for managing information hazards in other dual-use technologies.

Everything New in iOS 26.1 Beta 2

Apple has released the second beta of iOS 26.1 and iPadOS 26.1, introducing a range of useful changes and refinements to the operating systems. Notable additions include a "slide to stop" feature for alarms, the ability to create custom workouts in the Fitness app, and the reintroduction of Slide Over multitasking on iPad. The update also brings improvements like an automatic security update toggle, various UI alignment tweaks across apps, and subtle refinements to the Liquid Glass design.
