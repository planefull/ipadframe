Ideas: More AI-resilient biosecurity with the Paraphrase Project

The "Paraphrase Project" uncovered a "zero day" vulnerability where open-source AI tools could redesign toxic proteins, such as ricin, to bypass existing biosecurity screening systems used by DNA synthesis companies. A cross-sector team then developed and deployed more robust screening tools, effectively patching this AI-enabled biosecurity gap. This effort demonstrated the dual-use nature of AI in biology and highlighted the critical need for proactive, collaborative, and adaptable strategies—encompassing technical solutions, policy, and responsible information-sharing—to mitigate catastrophic biosecurity risks.

Apple Opens MLS Playoff Matches to All Apple TV Subscribers

Apple will stream all MLS playoff matches to every Apple TV subscriber at no additional charge, eliminating the prior requirement for the separate MLS Season Pass. This expanded access is likely a strategy to convert casual viewers into paying Season Pass customers for the 2026 regular season, especially after recent price reductions for the add-on. The announcement follows Apple's recent deal to stream Formula 1, where Apple TV subscribers will also receive F1 TV Premium content for free, indicating a shift in their sports streaming strategy.
