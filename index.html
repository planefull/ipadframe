RenderFormer: How neural networks are reshaping 3D rendering

Microsoft Research has developed RenderFormer, a new neural architecture that fully replaces traditional 3D rendering processes with machine learning. Unlike previous methods, RenderFormer can render arbitrary 3D scenes with complex geometry, materials, and global illumination without relying on physics-based computations like ray tracing. This advancement marks a significant step toward general-purpose neural rendering, paving the way for future integration with broader AI systems and visual computing breakthroughs.

iPhone 17 Pro vs. iPhone 17 Pro Max: What's Exclusive to Apple's Largest Flagship iPhone

Despite initial rumors suggesting exclusive features like an upgraded Telephoto camera for the iPhone 17 Pro Max, both Pro models share the same general feature set this year. The primary differences between the iPhone 17 Pro and Pro Max are related to size, influencing the display, battery, and storage options. The Pro Max exclusively offers a larger 6.9-inch display, longer battery life (up to 39 hours of video playback), and an option for 2TB of storage, whereas the Pro is limited to 1TB.
