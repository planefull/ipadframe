When industry knowledge meets PIKE-RAG: The innovation behind Signify’s customer service boost

Signify, a leader in LED lighting, faced significant challenges in efficiently providing accurate answers to complex technical inquiries due to vast product data and diverse documentation formats. To address this, they partnered with Microsoft Research Asia to integrate PIKE-RAG technology into their Azure-based knowledge management system. This integration led to a 12% improvement in answer accuracy by enabling multimodal document parsing, eliminating reliance on erroneous data sources, and performing dynamic multi-hop reasoning for complex questions.
