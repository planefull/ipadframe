When industry knowledge meets PIKE-RAG: The innovation behind Signify’s customer service boost

Signify, a leader in LED lighting, struggled to provide accurate and efficient technical answers due to its vast, complex product documentation and diverse data formats. This collaboration resulted in a 12% improvement in answer accuracy by enabling the system to understand multimodal content, rely on original documents, and perform multi-hop reasoning for complex inquiries, demonstrating PIKE-RAG's strong capabilities for industrial knowledge management.
