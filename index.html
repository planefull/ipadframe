Tell me when: Building agents that can wait, monitor, and act

Modern LLM agents struggle with long-running monitoring tasks, such as monitoring email or price drops, because they lack the ability to "wait" efficiently, leading to either excessive checking or giving up. To address this, Microsoft Research introduced SentinelStep, a mechanism that wraps agents in a workflow with dynamic polling and careful context management, enabling them to monitor conditions for extended periods. Integrated into their Magentic-UI prototype, SentinelStep significantly improves the reliability of long-duration tasks, paving the way for more patient and proactive AI assistants.
