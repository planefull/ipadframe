Using AI to assist in rare disease diagnosis

Microsoft Research, in collaboration with Drexel University and the Broad Institute, conducted a study exploring how generative AI can assist genetic professionals in diagnosing rare diseases through whole genome sequencing. The research identified challenges such as information overload, inefficient collaboration, and difficulty prioritizing reanalysis of unsolved cases. They designed an AI assistant prototype to address these by flagging cases for reanalysis based on new scientific findings and synthesizing vast amounts of gene and variant information, ultimately aiming to improve diagnostic efficiency and patient outcomes.

Amazon Unveils New Echo Speakers with Alexa+ AI and Omnisense Technology

Amazon has unveiled new Echo Dot Max, Echo Studio, Echo Show 8, and Echo Show 11 smart speakers, all powered by the new Alexa+ AI assistant. These devices feature faster chips with AI acceleration and a custom Omnisense sensor platform, designed for ambient AI capabilities like personalized reminders and home monitoring. The updated lineup boasts improved audio, revamped designs, enhanced screens and cameras for the Echo Show models, and positions Amazon to further compete in the smart home market against rivals like Apple while expanding Alexa+ integration with other brands.
