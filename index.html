Tell me when: Building agents that can wait, monitor, and act

Modern LLM agents struggle with long-running monitoring tasks, such like tracking emails or price changes, because they lack the ability to intelligently determine *when* to check, leading to inefficiency or failure. To address this, Microsoft Research introduced SentinelStep, a mechanism integrated into Magentic-UI that enables agents to perform these tasks by wrapping them in a workflow with dynamic polling and careful context management. This approach significantly improves task reliability over extended periods by adjusting polling frequency and preventing context overflow, paving the way for more practical, proactive, and persistent AI assistants.
