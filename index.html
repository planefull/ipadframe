MMCTAgent: Enabling multimodal reasoning over large video and image collections

Modern multimodal AI models struggle with complex, long-form visual data, lacking the temporal and iterative reasoning necessary for real-world tasks. To address this, Microsoft Research developed MMCTAgent, a Multi-modal Critical Thinking Agent utilizing a Planner-Critic architecture built on AutoGen. This agent employs modality-specific tools and iterative reasoning, significantly enhancing performance and explainability for analyzing large collections of images and long-form videos.
