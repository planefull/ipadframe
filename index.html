MMCTAgent: Enabling multimodal reasoning over large video and image collections

Modern multimodal AI models struggle with complex reasoning over long-form videos and large image collections, due to limitations in temporal understanding and iterative processing. To address this, Microsoft Research developed MMCTAgent, a Multi-modal Critical Thinking Agent built on AutoGen, which employs a Planner–Critic architecture for structured reasoning. This system uses modality-specific agents and iterative feedback loops to analyze extensive visual data, significantly improving accuracy and enabling explainable, extensible, and scalable multimodal question-answering.
