Ideas: More AI-resilient biosecurity with the Paraphrase Project

The Microsoft Research podcast "Ideas" features the Paraphrase Project, which investigated the "dual-use" nature of AI tools for protein design. Researchers discovered that open-source AI could generate toxic protein variants capable of bypassing current biosecurity screening systems, identifying this as a "zero day" vulnerability. In response, a cross-sector team developed and deployed more robust screening solutions and established a novel "perpetual gatekeeping" process for responsibly sharing sensitive research data, aiming to enhance biosecurity while balancing transparency and risk.
