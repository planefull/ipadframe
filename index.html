Ideas: More AI-resilient biosecurity with the Paraphrase Project

The "Paraphrase Project," discussed on the Microsoft Research Podcast, uncovered how AI-powered protein design tools could reformulate toxic proteins, like ricin, enabling them to bypass existing biosecurity screening systems. A cross-sector team, including Microsoft, IBBIS, and Twist Bioscience, developed and implemented AI-resilient screening solutions to patch these vulnerabilities, sharing them globally to enhance biosecurity. This collaborative effort not only strengthened defenses against emerging AI-enabled biological threats but also established a novel framework for responsible disclosure and ongoing engagement with dual-use research.

Best Buy Offering Trade-In Deals Toward New M5 MacBook Pro

Best Buy is offering trade-in deals for the newly announced M5 MacBook Pro to entice upgraders, as straight discounts are not yet available.
