Ideas: More AI-resilient biosecurity with the Paraphrase Project

The Paraphrase Project uncovered a "zero-day" vulnerability where open-source AI tools could redesign toxic proteins, like ricin, to evade existing biosecurity screening systems used by DNA synthesis companies. A cross-sector team, including Microsoft Research, Twist Bioscience, and IBBIS, responded by developing and deploying "patches" – more robust AI-resilient screening solutions – to strengthen global biosecurity. This effort also pioneered a new perpetual gatekeeping strategy for sensitive scientific data and methods, demonstrating a proactive approach to managing dual-use AI risks in biotechnology.

Review: Anker's New Nano Line is Worth Checking Out

Anker's new "Nano" line includes an ultra-slim magnetic power bank, a 10,000mAh power bank with a retractable USB-C cable, and a versatile desktop charging station. The MagGo Nano Power Bank is highly praised for its exceptionally thin design, while the Nano Charging Station stands out for its integrated retractable cables, multiple ports, and surge protection, making it ideal for desk organization. Overall, the products are considered well-designed, useful, and competitively priced, earning a strong recommendation from the reviewer.
