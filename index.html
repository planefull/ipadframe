Using AI to assist in rare disease diagnosis

Microsoft Research, in collaboration with Drexel University and the Broad Institute, conducted a study on using generative AI to assist genetic professionals in diagnosing rare diseases. The study identified challenges like information overload, inefficient collaboration, and difficulty prioritizing reanalysis in whole genome sequencing. They co-designed an AI assistant prototype to flag cases for reanalysis based on new scientific findings and to synthesize vast amounts of gene and variant information, aiming to improve diagnostic yield and shorten diagnosis times through expert-AI collaboration.

Apple Releases iOS 26.0.1 With Fixes for Wi-Fi, Cellular, and Camera Issues on iPhone 17 Models

Apple has released iOS 26.0.1 and iPadOS 26.0.1, the first updates to its recently launched operating systems. These updates primarily address critical bug fixes for various iPhone 17 models, including camera aberrations, cellular and Wi-Fi connectivity issues, and problems with tinted app icons. Both updates also fix VoiceOver issues, with iPadOS additionally resolving an unexpected floating keyboard movement, and are available for over-the-air download.
