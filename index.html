When industry knowledge meets PIKE-RAG: The innovation behind Signify’s customer service boost

Signify, a leader in LED lighting, struggled with efficiently providing accurate answers to complex professional inquiries due to vast, multimodal product data and technical documentation. To address this, they partnered with Microsoft Research Asia to integrate PIKE-RAG technology into their knowledge management system built on Microsoft Azure. This advanced RAG system improved answer accuracy by 12% by better understanding multimodal content, utilizing an end-to-end knowledge loop, and performing dynamic multi-hop reasoning, offering a significant boost to customer service.

Apple Releases First iOS 26.2 and iPadOS 26.2 Public Betas

Apple has released the first public betas for iOS 26.2, iPadOS 26.2, tvOS 26.2, and watchOS 26.2, allowing public testers to download and try the upcoming updates. These betas introduce several new features, such as a Liquid Glass slider for the Lock Screen and changes to the Sleep Score feature. Additional updates include AirPods Live Translation in the EU, an alarm option for Reminders, and enhancements to apps like Podcasts, Passwords, and Freeform.
