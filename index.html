Tell me when: Building agents that can wait, monitor, and act

Modern LLM agents struggle with long-running monitoring tasks like tracking emails or price changes because they don't know *when* to check, leading to wasted resources or early failure. To address this, Microsoft Research developed SentinelStep, a mechanism that enables agents to handle such tasks by dynamically adjusting polling intervals and carefully managing context to prevent overflow. Integrated into the Magentic-UI prototype, SentinelStep significantly improves agent reliability for tasks lasting hours, marking a step towards more practical and proactive AI assistants.
