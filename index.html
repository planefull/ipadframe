MMCTAgent: Enabling multimodal reasoning over large video and image collections

Modern multimodal AI models struggle with long-form and large-scale visual data, which demands complex temporal and cross-modal reasoning beyond their current capabilities. To address this, Microsoft Research developed MMCTAgent, a Multi-modal Critical Thinking Agent built on AutoGen, designed for structured reasoning over extensive video and image collections. MMCTAgent utilizes a Planner-Critic architecture and modality-specific agents with specialized tools to perform iterative reasoning, significantly improving performance on benchmarks by enabling planning, reflection, and tool-based analysis for accurate and explainable insights.

Matter 1.5 Launches With Camera Support

The Connectivity Standards Alliance has released Matter 1.5, the latest version of its cross-platform smart home standard. This update introduces support for cameras, closures like window shades and garage doors, and soil sensors for automating plant care, enabling manufacturers to develop new types of Matter-enabled devices. Additionally, Matter 1.5 enhances energy management features, providing real-time pricing and carbon data to devices for more accurate cost and consumption tracking.
