iOS 26.2 Will Bring 10+ New Features to Your iPhone

General enhancements include alarm options for Reminders, a customizable Liquid Glass Lock Screen, temporary AirDrop codes for non-contacts, offline Apple Music lyrics, and improved Sleep Score metrics.
