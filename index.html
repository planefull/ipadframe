When industry knowledge meets PIKE-RAG: The innovation behind Signify’s customer service boost

Signify, a leader in connected LED lighting, struggled to efficiently provide accurate answers from its extensive, complex, and multimodal product documentation to professional users. To overcome this, they partnered with Microsoft Research Asia to integrate PIKE-RAG technology into their Microsoft Azure-based knowledge management system. This implementation resulted in a 12% improvement in answer accuracy by enabling multimodal document parsing, eliminating reliance on erroneous data sources, and performing dynamic task decomposition with multi-hop reasoning for complex queries.
