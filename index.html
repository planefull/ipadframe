Ideas: More AI-resilient biosecurity with the Paraphrase Project

The Microsoft Research Podcast episode discusses the "Paraphrase Project," which investigated the dual-use nature of AI-powered protein design, revealing that AI tools could create novel toxic proteins capable of bypassing existing biosecurity screening systems. Researchers from Microsoft, IBBIS, and Twist Bioscience demonstrated this vulnerability by generating AI-rewritten versions of toxic proteins that went undetected by current safeguards. This led to a "cybersecurity-style" response, resulting in the development and global implementation of more robust, AI-resilient screening solutions and a new perpetual gatekeeping strategy for handling sensitive dual-use information in scientific publications.
