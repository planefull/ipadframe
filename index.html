Ideas: More AI-resilient biosecurity with the Paraphrase Project

The Paraphrase Project, a collaborative effort by Microsoft Research and partners, uncovered a "zero day" vulnerability where AI protein design tools could reformulate toxic proteins to bypass existing biosecurity screening systems. To address this dual-use challenge, a cross-sector team developed a red-teaming approach, created more robust protein screening systems, and implemented a novel perpetual gatekeeping strategy for responsible data and software sharing. This initiative demonstrates a proactive model for identifying and mitigating AI-enabled biosecurity risks, emphasizing the need for ongoing vigilance and collaboration across industry, government, and nonprofits.
