Using AI to assist in rare disease diagnosis

Microsoft Research, in collaboration with Drexel University and the Broad Institute, conducted a study to address challenges faced by genetic professionals in diagnosing rare diseases through whole genome sequencing. They co-designed a generative AI assistant to help synthesize vast amounts of genetic data, flag cases for reanalysis, and overcome information overload and collaboration difficulties. This AI-enhanced tool aims to streamline workflows, improve diagnostic yield, and reduce the time it takes to diagnose rare genetic diseases, ultimately improving patient outcomes.
