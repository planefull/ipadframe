Another Designer Leaves Apple

Abidur Chowdhury, a rising star designer who introduced the iPhone Air, has departed Apple to join an AI startup, marking a significant internal event. His departure continues a trend of several high-profile designers leaving Apple since Jony Ive's exit in 2019, leaving few remaining from that era. This shift comes as the design team's former oversight, Jeff Williams, also retired, leading to the team now reporting directly to Apple CEO Tim Cook.
