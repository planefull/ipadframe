MMCTAgent: Enabling multimodal reasoning over large video and image collections

Modern multimodal AI struggles to analyze long-form video and large image collections, lacking advanced temporal reasoning and iterative refinement capabilities. To address this, Microsoft Research developed MMCTAgent, a Multi-modal Critical Thinking Agent built on AutoGen, featuring a Planner-Critic architecture. This system uses specialized agents and tools for structured ingestion and iterative reasoning, enabling accurate, explainable, and scalable analysis of complex visual data, significantly outperforming base models.
