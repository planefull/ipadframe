Ideas: More AI-resilient biosecurity with the Paraphrase Project

The Microsoft Research podcast episode "Ideas: More AI-resilient biosecurity with the Paraphrase Project" discusses the dual-use nature of AI-powered protein design, highlighting how these tools can inadvertently enable the redesign of toxic proteins to bypass current biosecurity screening systems. The Paraphrase Project, a collaborative effort, demonstrated this vulnerability by creating thousands of "paraphrased" toxic proteins that evaded existing defenses, prompting a cybersecurity-style "zero-day" response. This led to the development and global dissemination of enhanced screening methods, strengthening biosecurity and pioneering a new perpetual gatekeeping strategy for responsible sharing of sensitive research data to mitigate future risks.

Everything New in iOS 26.1 Beta 2

Apple has released iOS 26.1 and iPadOS 26.1 beta 2, introducing a range of functional and visual improvements. Key iOS changes include a new "slide to stop" gesture for alarms, a toggle for automatic security improvement downloads, and the ability to create custom workouts in the Fitness app. iPadOS 26.1 reintroduces the Slide Over multitasking feature and adds external microphone gain control, alongside various interface alignment tweaks and subtle Liquid Glass design refinements across both platforms.
