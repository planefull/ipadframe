Ideas: More AI-resilient biosecurity with the Paraphrase Project

The Paraphrase Project revealed a significant biosecurity vulnerability where AI tools could redesign toxic proteins, such as ricin, to evade existing screening systems used by DNA synthesis companies. In response, a cross-sector team rapidly developed and deployed "patches" that enhanced these screening systems, making them more resilient against AI-generated variants. This pioneering work also established a novel perpetual gatekeeping strategy for managing sensitive scientific information, underscoring the ongoing need for proactive, collaborative defenses against the dual-use challenges of AI in biology.

Samsung to Unveil Apple Vision Pro Competitor Tomorrow

Samsung is holding a Galaxy Event tomorrow to unveil its "Project Moohan" mixed reality headset, positioned as a direct competitor to the Apple Vision Pro.
