ChatGPT Group Chats Now Available Worldwide

OpenAI has officially rolled out group chats in ChatGPT worldwide, allowing multiple people to collaborate with the AI within a single shared conversation. Users can create a dedicated group space, invite others via a link, and leverage ChatGPT for diverse tasks such as trip planning, design collaboration, or research. ChatGPT participates intelligently, can be prompted directly, and this feature is now available to all logged-in users across all plans.
