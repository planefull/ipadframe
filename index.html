Using AI to assist in rare disease diagnosis

Diagnosing rare genetic diseases is challenging due to the immense and complex data from whole genome sequencing, leading to low initial diagnosis rates and time-consuming reanalysis. To address this, Microsoft Research, Drexel University, and the Broad Institute developed and studied a generative AI assistant designed to support genetic professionals in synthesizing vast biomedical data. This AI tool aims to enhance diagnostic accuracy and efficiency by flagging cases for reanalysis and aggregating critical gene and variant information, ultimately improving patient outcomes for rare diseases.

Everything New in iOS 26.1 Beta 1

Apple has released the first beta of iOS 26.1, just a week after the launch of iOS 26, bringing several new features and improvements. The update primarily expands language support for Apple Intelligence and AirPods Live Translation, adding various languages like Danish, Japanese, and Chinese. Additionally, it introduces UI refinements to apps such as Phone, Apple Music, Photos, and Calendar, along with a renamed security feature now called "Background Security Improvement."
