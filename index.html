Ideas: More AI-resilient biosecurity with the Paraphrase Project

The Microsoft Research *Ideas* podcast episode discusses the "Paraphrase Project," which investigated AI's dual-use potential in biosecurity. Researchers demonstrated that AI could redesign toxic protein sequences, like ricin, in ways that evaded existing biosecurity screening systems, prompting a "zero day" cybersecurity-style response. This collaborative effort led to the development of more robust, AI-resilient protein screening solutions shared globally and established a novel "perpetual gatekeeping" approach for responsibly managing sensitive scientific information.

Apple Seeds Third Betas of iOS 26.1, iPadOS 26.1, macOS Tahoe 26.1 and More
