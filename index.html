Tell me when: Building agents that can wait, monitor, and act

Current LLM agents struggle with long-running monitoring tasks like tracking emails or price changes, failing because they don't know *when* to check, leading to wasted resources or giving up. To solve this, Microsoft Research developed SentinelStep, a mechanism that enables agents to efficiently monitor conditions for hours or days by dynamically adjusting polling intervals and managing context within a workflow. Integrated into their open-source Magentic-UI system, SentinelStep significantly improves task reliability for longer durations, paving the way for more proactive and resource-efficient AI assistants.

Apple CEO Tim Cook Joins President Trump in Tokyo

Apple CEO Tim Cook joined President Donald Trump and other U.S. and Japanese technology executives at a closed-door dinner in Tokyo. The event aimed to promote Japanese investment in the United States, with President Trump highlighting a $550 billion framework and potential new commitments, paired with tariff concessions. While many prominent business leaders attended, Cook did not speak, and no Apple-specific announcements were made.
