Ideas: More AI-resilient biosecurity with the Paraphrase Project

The Paraphrase Project explores the dual-use nature of AI in protein design, revealing that AI tools can reformulate toxic proteins to bypass existing biosecurity screening systems, creating a "zero-day" vulnerability. A cross-sector team conducted a cybersecurity-style red-teaming effort, successfully developing more robust protein screening systems and sharing these "patches" globally through the International Gene Synthesis Consortium. This project also pioneered a "perpetual gatekeeping" strategy for sensitive research, demonstrating a new model for balancing open science with the need to mitigate catastrophic biological risks in the age of advanced AI.
