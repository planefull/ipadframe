Tell me when: Building agents that can wait, monitor, and act

Modern LLM agents struggle with long-running monitoring tasks, such as tracking emails or price changes, because they lack the ability to efficiently manage polling frequency and context over extended periods. To address this, Microsoft Research introduced SentinelStep, a mechanism integrated into their Magentic-UI agentic system, designed to enable agents to patiently monitor conditions for hours or days. SentinelStep achieves this by dynamically adjusting polling intervals based on the task and carefully managing agent state to prevent context overflow, significantly improving the reliability of agents on longer-duration tasks.
