Using AI to assist in rare disease diagnosis

Genetic professionals face significant challenges in accurately interpreting vast whole genome sequencing data, leading to low diagnosis rates and lengthy reanalysis processes for rare genetic diseases. To address this, Microsoft Research, Drexel University, and the Broad Institute designed a generative AI assistant to enhance "sensemaking" during analysis. This AI tool aims to streamline workflows by synthesizing complex biomedical information, flagging cases for reanalysis, and improving collaborative efforts, ultimately boosting diagnoses and improving patient quality of life.

Apple Store Moving Soon at University Park Village in Fort Worth, Texas

Apple's retail store at University Park Village in Fort Worth, Texas, is relocating within the shopping center to a new, larger space. The updated store is set to open on Saturday, October 11, at 10 a.m. local time, on the north side of the village. This new location, previously occupied by Jos. A. Bank and The Impeccable Pig, will offer Apple a significantly larger footprint and an updated design compared to its original 2008 store.
