Crescent library brings privacy to digital identity systems

Digital identities, though convenient, introduce privacy risks like "linkability," where hidden identifiers can connect separate uses of credentials to build user profiles. To counter this, Microsoft Research developed Crescent, a cryptographic library that adds "unlinkability" to common identity formats like JSON Web Tokens and mobile driver's licenses. Crescent uses zero-knowledge proofs to ensure that separate digital interactions cannot be linked, enhancing privacy without requiring changes to existing credential issuance systems.

Two Apple Stores Open in India This Week Ahead of iPhone 17 Launch

Apple is opening two new retail stores in India this week: Apple Koregaon Park in Pune and Apple Hebbal in Bengaluru, expanding its presence to five locations in the country. Apple Koregaon Park, previewed today and opening September 4, features a combined Genius Bar and Apple Pickup area, similar to its new counterpart. These store openings come ahead of the iPhone 17 launch, underscoring India's growing significance as a key market for Apple's sales and manufacturing.
