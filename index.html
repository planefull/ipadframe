Applicability vs. job displacement: further notes on our recent research on AI and occupations

Microsoft Research clarified their recent paper on the occupational implications of generative AI, which studied where AI chatbots might be useful. The research found AI is most applicable for knowledge work and communication tasks like writing and information gathering, suggesting it can assist in specific subtasks within occupations. They explicitly stated that the study does not conclude jobs will be eliminated, but rather focuses on AI's potential as a tool to improve workflows, acknowledging the limitations of equating task applicability with overall job performance.
