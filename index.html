Crescent library brings privacy to digital identity systems

Digital identities, while convenient, introduce privacy risks through "linkability," allowing hidden identifiers to track user behavior across various digital interactions. To counter this, Microsoft Research has developed Crescent, a cryptographic library that adds "unlinkability" to common identity formats like JSON Web Tokens and mobile driver's licenses. Crescent utilizes zero-knowledge proofs and a two-stage process to enable users to selectively share information without revealing underlying data or allowing separate credential presentations to be linked, thus significantly enhancing privacy.

Vision Pro and HomePod Mini Rumored to Come in These New Colors

According to a report by Bloomberg's Mark Gurman, Apple may introduce new color options for its Vision Pro and HomePod mini devices. Gurman suggests a Space Black color for the Vision Pro and new colors, including Red, for the HomePod mini, which currently comes in a range of brighter hues.
