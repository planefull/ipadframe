Apple Hit With $634 Million Verdict in Apple Watch Blood Oxygen Patent Lawsuit

A U.S. federal jury has ordered Apple to pay medical technology company Masimo $634 million for infringing patents related to the blood oxygen-sensing technology in the Apple Watch. Apple plans to appeal the verdict, arguing the patent is invalid and expired, while Masimo considers this a significant win for protecting its intellectual property. This patent dispute previously caused a U.S. sales ban on certain Apple Watch models, leading Apple to implement a software workaround that Masimo has since challenged with another lawsuit.
