Ideas: More AI-resilient biosecurity with the Paraphrase Project

The Paraphrase Project, discussed in this Microsoft Research podcast, addresses the "dual-use" nature of AI in synthetic biology, specifically how AI tools can redesign toxic proteins to bypass current biosecurity screening systems. Researchers demonstrated this "zero-day" vulnerability by creating AI-paraphrased versions of toxic proteins that evaded detection by major DNA synthesis companies. The team then developed and shared "patches" to strengthen these screening systems and established a new "perpetual gatekeeping" strategy for scientific publications to responsibly disclose sensitive information while mitigating misuse risks.

macOS 26.1 Beta Hints at Pro Display XDR 2 With Center Stage Camera

Apple's latest macOS 26.1 beta hints at a forthcoming Pro Display XDR 2, featuring a built-in camera. Code strings like "Pro Display XDR Camera" suggest the new monitor will integrate a Center Stage camera, enabling features like Desk View.
