Ideas: More AI-resilient biosecurity with the Paraphrase Project

The Microsoft Research "Paraphrase Project" explored the dual-use nature of AI by demonstrating how AI-powered protein design tools could reformulate toxic proteins to bypass current biosecurity screening systems. This discovery, deemed a "zero-day" vulnerability for AI and biosecurity, prompted a cross-sector collaboration to develop and implement more robust, AI-resilient screening solutions. The project also pioneered a novel "perpetual gatekeeping" approach for responsibly sharing sensitive research details, balancing open science with the need to mitigate information hazards in dual-use technologies.

5+ New Features Your iPhone Will Get in iOS 26.1

Apple is currently beta testing iOS 26.1, the first major update to iOS 26, which introduces various new features and interface changes without including the anticipated new Siri. Key additions include a "slide to stop" gesture for alarms, expanded language support for Apple Intelligence and AirPods Live Translation, and custom workout creation in the Fitness app. The update also brings visual refinements to the Settings app, Home Screen folders, and Photos app, while iPadOS 26.1 reintroduces Slide Over functionality.
