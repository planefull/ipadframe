Ideas: More AI-resilient biosecurity with the Paraphrase Project

The Microsoft Research Podcast *Ideas* explores the "Paraphrase Project," which investigated the dual-use nature of AI in protein design and its impact on biosecurity. Researchers discovered that AI tools could "paraphrase" toxic protein sequences, making them undetectable by existing DNA synthesis screening systems, a vulnerability dubbed the first "zero day" in AI biosecurity. A cross-sector team developed red-teaming approaches, created and implemented "patches" to strengthen screening, and established a novel "perpetual gatekeeping" method for responsible data sharing to mitigate future risks while promoting open science.

M5 MacBook Air Coming Spring 2026 With M5 Mac Studio and Mac Mini in Development
