MMCTAgent: Enabling multimodal reasoning over large video and image collections

Microsoft Research developed MMCTAgent (Multi-modal Critical Thinking Agent) to overcome existing multimodal AI limitations in analyzing long-form video and large image collections, which often require complex reasoning beyond simple object recognition. Built on Microsoft's AutoGen, MMCTAgent uses a Planner-Critic architecture with modality-specific agents (ImageAgent and VideoAgent) and specialized tools to perform iterative, deliberate reasoning. This framework enables structured ingestion, retrieval, and refinement of information, significantly enhancing performance and providing explainable, scalable insights for diverse real-world applications.

Google Launches More Intelligent Gemini 3 Model

Google has launched Gemini 3 Pro, its newest and most intelligent AI model, which boasts state-of-the-art reasoning, superior multimodal understanding, and better context comprehension. This advanced model, designed for concise responses and outperforming previous versions, is rolling out across various Google platforms including AI Mode in Search and the redesigned Gemini app. Additionally, a more powerful Gemini 3 Deep Think mode will soon be available to Google AI Ultra subscribers for even more complex problem-solving.
