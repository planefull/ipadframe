MMCTAgent: Enabling multimodal reasoning over large video and image collections

Modern multimodal AI models struggle with long-form video and large image libraries, lacking the temporal reasoning and iterative refinement needed for real-world scenarios. To address this, Microsoft Research developed MMCTAgent (Multi-modal Critical Thinking Agent), an AutoGen-based system featuring a Planner-Critic architecture for structured reasoning. This agent uses specialized tools and iterative feedback loops to analyze complex queries across vast visual data, significantly improving accuracy and interpretability compared to conventional one-shot models.
