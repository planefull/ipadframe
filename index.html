Ideas: More AI-resilient biosecurity with the Paraphrase Project

The "Paraphrase Project" uncovered a critical "zero day" vulnerability where AI tools could redesign toxic proteins, such as ricin, to evade existing biosecurity screening systems. A collaborative cross-sector team used a "red-teaming" approach to develop and implement more robust, AI-resilient protein screening software. This initiative resulted in global sharing of updated screening "patches" and established a novel "perpetual gatekeeping" strategy for the responsible disclosure of sensitive dual-use research.
