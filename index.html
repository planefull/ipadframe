What's New With Multitasking in iPadOS 26.2

The third beta of iPadOS 26.2 brings significant updates to iPad multitasking, largely improving the functionality of Slide Over. This feature, which returned in iPadOS 26.1, now works more like its iPadOS 18 counterpart, alongside new options for replacing apps in tiled views. Users can drag and drop apps from sources like the Dock or App Library into various multitasking layouts, including tiled views or Slide Over, and even easily replace existing apps by dragging new ones over them.
