Ideas: More AI-resilient biosecurity with the Paraphrase Project

The "Paraphrase Project" identified a critical biosecurity vulnerability where AI tools could redesign toxic proteins, like ricin, to evade existing screening systems used by DNA synthesis companies. A cross-sector team then developed and shared "patches" to enhance these protein screening systems globally, effectively addressing this "zero day" threat. This collaborative effort also pioneered a new model for responsible disclosure of AI-related biosecurity risks, including perpetual gatekeeping for sensitive data, to guide future responses to dual-use technology dilemmas.

Apple Releases New Beta Firmware for AirPods Pro 2, AirPods Pro 3 and AirPods 4

Apple has released new beta firmware (build 8B5014c) for the AirPods Pro 2, AirPods Pro 3, and AirPods 4, which is currently limited to developers. This firmware likely adds support for Live Translation in new languages, such as Japanese, Korean, Italian, and Chinese, as part of the iOS 26.1 update. Developers can now easily install beta firmware through a dedicated option in the AirPods settings interface on their connected iPhone, iPad, or Mac.
