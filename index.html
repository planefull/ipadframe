Ideas: More AI-resilient biosecurity with the Paraphrase Project

The Microsoft Research "Paraphrase Project" investigated how AI-powered protein design tools could be misused to create toxic proteins that evade current biosecurity screening systems, revealing a significant "zero day" vulnerability. A cross-sector team developed a cybersecurity-style response, patching screening tools to make them more AI-resilient and demonstrating their effectiveness on a broad set of hazardous proteins. This effort culminated in a new model for responsible disclosure and data sharing, involving a "perpetual gatekeeping" process to navigate information hazards and enhance global biosecurity in the face of rapidly advancing AI.

Apple TV's 'Vibrant' Rebranding Starts to Appear in Latest iOS 26.1 Beta

Apple has announced the rebranding of Apple TV+ to simply "Apple TV," promising a "vibrant new identity" for the service. Hints of this change are already visible in the latest iOS 26.1 and tvOS 26.1 betas, where app icons and visuals have adopted a glass-like multicolor design, replacing the prior subdued gray theme. While the rebrand is evident on Apple's press pages, it has not yet propagated to consumer-focused sites, and it remains to be seen if additional components beyond the name and logo will be part of the update.
