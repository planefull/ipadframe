Applicability vs. job displacement: further notes on our recent research on AI and occupations

Microsoft Research's paper analyzed how generative AI, specifically Bing Copilot, could assist various occupations, finding it most useful for knowledge-based and communication tasks like writing and information gathering. The authors clarify that their study does not conclude or suggest that AI will eliminate jobs, directly addressing misinterpretations of their findings. Instead, the research highlights AI's potential to improve workflows by assisting with subtasks, while acknowledging the limitations of applying AI capabilities to the full, nuanced scope of human occupations.
