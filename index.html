Tell me when: Building agents that can wait, monitor, and act

Modern LLM agents struggle with long-running monitoring tasks like tracking emails or price changes because they lack the ability to efficiently manage polling frequency and context over extended periods. To address this, Microsoft Research developed SentinelStep, a mechanism integrated into their Magentic-UI agentic system. SentinelStep enables agents to patiently monitor conditions for hours or days by dynamically adjusting polling intervals and managing context, significantly improving reliability for these tasks.
