When industry knowledge meets PIKE-RAG: The innovation behind Signify’s customer service boost

Signify, a global leader in LED lighting, faced challenges in providing accurate and efficient answers to professional users due to complex product data across diverse, multimodal documents. To address this, they partnered with Microsoft Research Asia to implement PIKE-RAG technology into their Azure-based knowledge management system. Apple Debuts iPhone Pocket, a Limited Edition iPod Sock-Style Accessory

Apple has collaborated with Japanese fashion house ISSEY MIYAKE to launch "iPhone Pocket," a limited-edition, 3D-knitted accessory designed to stylishly carry an iPhone, AirPods, and other small items. Inspired by "a piece of cloth," this stretchy, ribbed textile pocket features an elongated strap that fully encloses the iPhone while allowing the display to be glimpsed. Available in short and long strap versions with various color options, priced from $149.95 to $229.95, it will be released on November 14th in select countries and flagship Apple Stores.
