Tell me when: Building agents that can wait, monitor, and act

Modern LLM agents struggle with long-running monitoring tasks like tracking emails or price changes because they lack the ability to intelligently manage polling frequency and context over time. To address this, Microsoft Research developed SentinelStep, a mechanism that wraps agents in a workflow with dynamic polling and careful context management, integrated into their Magentic-UI prototype. This innovation significantly improves the reliability of agents for tasks lasting hours or days, enabling them to proactively monitor conditions without wasting resources and laying the groundwork for more patient and efficient AI assistants.

iOS 26.1 Features: Everything New in iOS 26.1

iOS 26.1 is now available as a significant update, introducing several new features and addressing user complaints from the initial iOS 26 release. Key improvements include a new transparency toggle for Liquid Glass and the ability to disable the Lock Screen camera swipe gesture, resolving a common irritation. Additionally, the update expands language support for Apple Intelligence and AirPods Live Translation, adds custom workout creation in the Fitness app, and reintroduces Slide Over multitasking for iPadOS.
