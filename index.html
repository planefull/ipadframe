Another Designer Leaves Apple

Abidur Chowdhury, a rising star designer known for introducing the iPhone Air, has left Apple to join an AI startup, a move that reportedly caused internal "waves." His departure was unrelated to the iPhone Air's sales performance. This follows a trend of high-profile designers leaving Apple since Jony Ive's departure, and comes shortly after former operations chief Jeff Williams, who oversaw product design, retired, leaving the design team to report directly to CEO Tim Cook.
