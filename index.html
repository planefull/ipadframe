Applicability vs. job displacement: further notes on our recent research on AI and occupations

Microsoft Research clarified their recent paper, "Working with AI: Measuring the Occupational Implications of Generative AI," which aimed to identify where AI chatbots could be most useful across different occupations. The study found AI to be most applicable to knowledge work and communication tasks, like writing and gathering information, suggesting it as a tool to assist with specific subtasks and improve workflows. Crucially, the authors emphasized that their research explicitly *did not* conclude that jobs would be eliminated by AI, cautioning against such misinterpretations.

Apple Has Reportedly Considered Releasing iPhone 17 Air Bumper Case

Apple is reportedly considering releasing a bumper case for the upcoming iPhone 17 Air, according to Bloomberg's Mark Gurman.
