Happy 24th Anniversary, iPod

The first-generation iPod launched on November 10, 2001, amidst numerous rumors that often misidentified its true nature as a digital music player. Despite initial criticisms regarding its price, design, and Mac-only compatibility, the device quickly became immensely popular, revolutionizing portable music and kicking off Apple's tradition of product launch lines. The iPod line was officially discontinued in 2022, but its "spirit" and innovative legacy continue to influence current Apple products like the iPhone and HomePod.
