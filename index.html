Ideas: More AI-resilient biosecurity with the Paraphrase Project

The Paraphrase Project revealed a "zero-day" vulnerability where AI tools could redesign toxic proteins, allowing them to evade existing biosecurity screening systems used by DNA synthesis companies. A collaborative team developed an AI-powered red-teaming pipeline to expose these weaknesses and then implemented "patches" to create more robust, AI-resilient protein screening methods. This effort not only strengthened global biosecurity but also pioneered a new perpetual gatekeeping strategy for responsibly sharing sensitive research on dual-use technologies.

OpenAI's Sora AI Video App Hits 1 Million Downloads

OpenAI's Sora, an AI app for generating videos, has rapidly surpassed one million downloads since its release at the end of September. This milestone was achieved faster than ChatGPT, despite Sora being invite-only and currently limited to the U.S. and Canada. The app allows users to create AI-powered videos from their uploaded "cameos" using the advanced Sora 2 model, and despite some criticism, it remains highly popular and tops free app charts.
