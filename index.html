Tell me when: Building agents that can wait, monitor, and act

Modern LLM agents struggle with long-running monitoring tasks because they lack the ability to efficiently wait and know *when* to check for updates, leading to resource waste or task failure. To address this, Microsoft Research developed SentinelStep, a mechanism that wraps agents in a workflow with dynamic polling and careful context management. This enables agents to reliably monitor conditions for extended periods, significantly improving their success rates for long-duration tasks and paving the way for more practical, proactive AI assistants.

iOS 26.1 Coming Next Week: All the New Features

Apple is finalizing iOS 26.1, the first major update to iOS 26, with a public release anticipated early next week. This update introduces a toggle for Liquid Glass transparency, the ability to disable Lock Screen Camera swipe, and a "slide to stop" mechanism for alarms. It also brings custom workout creation to the Fitness app, new language support for Apple Intelligence and AirPods Live Translation, various UI refinements, and reintroduces Slide Over multitasking for iPadOS 26.1.
