Apple and OpenAI Must Face xAI Lawsuit Over Siri Integration

A Texas judge denied Apple and OpenAI's request to dismiss a lawsuit filed by Elon Musk's xAI, which accuses the companies of conspiring to dominate the AI market. xAI claims Apple's exclusive integration of ChatGPT into Siri unfairly disadvantages competing generative AI chatbots like Grok, limiting user choice and depriving them of crucial data. The lawsuit seeks to halt this alleged anti-competitive scheme and recover billions in damages from Apple and OpenAI.
