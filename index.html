Applicability vs. job displacement: further notes on our recent research on AI and occupations

Microsoft Research clarified their recent paper on the occupational implications of generative AI, aiming to correct misunderstandings about its scope and conclusions. The original study identified that AI chatbots are most useful for assisting with knowledge work and communication tasks, such as writing and information gathering, across various occupations. Importantly, the research explicitly states it does not conclude that AI will lead to job displacement, emphasizing that the findings focus on AI's applicability as a tool for subtasks rather than its ability to replace entire jobs.
