Breaking the networking wall in AI infrastructure 

AI system performance is increasingly limited by network and memory bottlenecks, caused by a fundamental trade-off in power, reliability, and reach within current "narrow-and-fast" copper and optical communication links. To address this, Microsoft has developed MOSAIC, a novel optical link technology that uses a "wide-and-slow" architecture with hundreds of parallel low-speed channels powered by microLEDs. This approach simultaneously achieves low power consumption, high reliability, and long reach (up to 50 meters), enabling significant energy savings, enhanced system resilience, and new, scalable AI infrastructure designs.

A19 vs. A19 Pro: iPhone 17 Chip Differences

Apple introduced A19 and A19 Pro chips for the iPhone 17 lineup, all built on an advanced 3-nanometer process, with the standard iPhone 17 using the A19 and the iPhone Air and iPhone 17 Pro models featuring variants of the A19 Pro. All chips include "Neural Accelerators" and an updated Neural Engine for AI performance, along with display and image signal processor enhancements. The A19 Pro, particularly in the iPhone 17 Pro models, boasts the fastest smartphone CPU and most advanced GPU, delivering up to 40% better sustained performance than its predecessor, aided by vapor chamber cooling and increased RAM in some devices.
