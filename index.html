Apple's 2026 Mac Plans

Apple is planning a significant refresh of its Mac lineup throughout 2026, with most models expected to receive M5 chips, and potentially the first M6 chips by year-end. Early 2026 updates include the MacBook Air and MacBook Pro, alongside the debut of a new, more affordable 13-inch MacBook featuring an A18 Pro chip. Later in the year, M5 Max/Ultra chips are slated for the Mac mini and Mac Studio, while a redesigned OLED MacBook Pro with a touchscreen and M6 chip is also a possibility.
