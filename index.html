Tell me when: Building agents that can wait, monitor, and act

Current LLM agents struggle with long-running monitoring tasks, such as tracking emails or price drops, because they lack the ability to intelligently wait and check at appropriate intervals. To address this, Microsoft Research developed SentinelStep, a mechanism that enables agents to effectively monitor conditions for extended periods by dynamically adjusting polling frequency and managing context to prevent overflow. Integrated into their Magentic-UI prototype, SentinelStep significantly improves the reliability of agents on longer tasks, laying the groundwork for more patient and proactive AI assistants.

iOS 26.1 Features: Everything New in iOS 26.1

iOS 26.1, the first major update to iOS 26, is now available with several new features and bug fixes. Key improvements include a Liquid Glass transparency toggle, the ability to disable the Lock Screen camera swipe, and a "slide to stop" feature for alarms to prevent accidental dismissals. Additionally, the update expands Apple Intelligence and AirPods Live Translation language support, introduces custom workouts in the Fitness app, and reintroduces Slide Over for enhanced multitasking on iPadOS 26.1.
