Tell me when: Building agents that can wait, monitor, and act

Modern LLM agents struggle with long-running monitoring tasks, such as tracking emails or price drops, because they lack the ability to know *when* to check, leading to inefficiency or failure. To solve this, Microsoft Research introduces SentinelStep, a mechanism that enables agents to patiently complete these tasks by wrapping them in a workflow that dynamically adjusts polling frequency and manages context to prevent overflow. Implemented in Magentic-UI, SentinelStep significantly improves agent reliability on longer tasks, laying the groundwork for more proactive, persistent, and resource-efficient AI assistants.
