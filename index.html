Using AI to assist in rare disease diagnosis

Microsoft Research, in collaboration with Drexel University and the Broad Institute, conducted a study to address the challenges genetic professionals face in diagnosing rare diseases through whole genome sequencing. They found that professionals struggle with information overload, inefficient collaboration, and prioritizing case reanalysis due to vast and complex data. To tackle these issues, they co-designed a generative AI assistant intended to streamline workflows by flagging cases for reanalysis and synthesizing scientific literature, aiming to increase diagnostic yield and reduce diagnosis time for rare genetic diseases.

iOS 26: Tint App Icons to Precisely Match Your iPhone Case Color

In iOS 26, Apple has introduced a new Home screen customization feature that allows app icons to precisely match the color of your iPhone's case. This option specifically works with official Apple MagSafe cases that include an NFC chip containing color variant information. Users can enable this "Tinted" mode through Home screen customization settings, which automatically adjusts icon colors for legibility while reflecting the case's tone.
