Applicability vs. job displacement: further notes on our recent research on AI and occupations

Microsoft Research clarified their recent paper, stating it identified occupations where AI chatbots could be useful for assisting with tasks, not that jobs would be eliminated. The research found AI most applicable to knowledge work and communication, such as writing and information gathering, rather than physical tasks. They emphasized that the study focused on AI as an assistive tool, acknowledging methodological limitations and cautioning against drawing conclusions about job displacement.
