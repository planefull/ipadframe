Ideas: More AI-resilient biosecurity with the Paraphrase Project

The Paraphrase Project uncovered a critical vulnerability where AI tools could redesign toxic proteins to bypass existing biosecurity screening systems, a discovery described as the first "zero day" in AI and biosecurity. A cross-sector team rapidly responded with a cybersecurity-style approach, developing and implementing more robust, AI-resilient protein screening systems to patch this threat. The project also introduced a novel "perpetual gatekeeping" strategy for sensitive data, setting a precedent for responsibly sharing scientific information to advance biosecurity while mitigating potential misuse of dual-use AI technologies.

Must-Try tvOS 26 Features

Apple has introduced tvOS 26 for the Apple TV, bringing several useful new features that often go under the radar compared to iOS updates. Key enhancements include an overhauled Apple TV app, easier profile switching for personalized content, and subtle Liquid Glass interface elements. Additionally, the update allows users to use their iPhone as a microphone for Apple Music Sing, set default third-party AirPlay speakers, and benefits from an updated FaceTime interface with live translation.
