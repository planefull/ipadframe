Crescent library brings privacy to digital identity systems

Digital identities, while convenient, introduce privacy risks like "linkability," where hidden identifiers can track users across various online activities. To combat this, Microsoft Research has developed Crescent, a cryptographic library that adds "unlinkability" to common identity formats such as JSON Web Tokens and mobile driver's licenses. Crescent achieves this by using zero-knowledge proofs and a two-stage process, enabling users to selectively disclose information and prevent linking of separate interactions without requiring changes to existing credential issuance systems.

Kuo: AirPods Pro 3 Coming Soon, But Bigger Upgrade Arriving Next Year

Apple analyst Ming-Chi Kuo reports that the AirPods Pro 3 are expected to be released in the second half of 2025, possibly at the September iPhone 17 event. These new earbuds are anticipated to feature heart rate monitoring, a smaller charging case, improved sound quality, and enhanced active noise cancellation. Kuo also predicts a more significant hardware upgrade in 2026, potentially including infrared cameras for advanced spatial audio with Apple Vision Pro and in-air gesture control.
