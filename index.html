Ideas: More AI-resilient biosecurity with the Paraphrase Project

The Paraphrase Project uncovered a critical "zero day" vulnerability where open-source AI tools could redesign toxic proteins, such as ricin, to bypass current biosecurity screening systems. In response, a cross-sector team developed an AI biosecurity red-teaming pipeline to identify these AI-generated variants and created "patches" — more robust protein screening systems — which have been shared globally through the International Gene Synthesis Consortium. This effort also pioneered a "perpetual gatekeeping" strategy for sensitive research data, balancing open science with the minimization of information hazards to proactively address the evolving dual-use challenges of AI in synthetic biology.

More Apple Apps Get Liquid Glass Redesign

Apple has updated its TestFlight and Apple Support apps, introducing new "Liquid Glass" designs to match the aesthetic of iOS 26. This redesign incorporates elements like rounded buttons, floating navigation bars, translucency, and layered app icons for both applications. Additionally, TestFlight received an icon overhaul, accessibility improvements, and a new Tester Matching feature to help users discover beta apps.
