Everything New in iOS 26.2 Beta 2

Apple has released iOS 26.2 beta 2 to developers, introducing a variety of new features and design refinements. Key updates include a "Liquid Glass" design for the Measure app's level, new sorting options by size in the Games app, and quicker, bouncier menu animations. The beta also provides more detailed information on Enhanced Safety Alerts, which are enabled by default to notify users of emergencies and can have improved delivery using approximate location data.
