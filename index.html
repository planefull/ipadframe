Tell me when: Building agents that can wait, monitor, and act

Current LLM agents struggle with long-running monitoring tasks like tracking emails or price drops because they lack the ability to wait efficiently without wasting resources or giving up. To address this, Microsoft Research introduces SentinelStep, a mechanism that enables agents to patiently monitor conditions for hours or days through dynamic polling and careful context management. Integrated into their Magentic-UI prototype, SentinelStep significantly improves reliability for extended tasks and is now open-sourced, paving the way for more practical, proactive, and always-on assistants.
