MMCTAgent: Enabling multimodal reasoning over large video and image collections

Modern multimodal AI models struggle with complex reasoning over long-form videos and large image libraries due to limitations in temporal context and iterative refinement. To address this, Microsoft Research developed MMCTAgent (Multi-modal Critical Thinking Agent), built on AutoGen, which employs a Planner-Critic architecture for structured reasoning. This system uses modality-specific agents and an iterative feedback loop to select appropriate tools, evaluate intermediate results, and refine conclusions, significantly enhancing accuracy and scalability for complex visual tasks.
