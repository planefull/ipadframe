Applicability vs. job displacement: further notes on our recent research on AI and occupations

Microsoft Research clarified its recent paper, stating its primary goal was to identify occupations where AI chatbots, like Bing Copilot, are most useful for assisting with specific tasks. The study found AI is most applicable to knowledge work and communication tasks, such as writing and information gathering, suggesting it can serve as a tool to enhance workflows. Crucially, the research explicitly *does not* conclude that AI will eliminate jobs, emphasizing that AI is intended to complement human strengths rather than replace entire occupations.
