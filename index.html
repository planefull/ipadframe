Apple Begins Selling Refurbished iPad Air With M3 Chip at a Discount

Apple has recently made iPad Air models with the M3 chip available in its refurbished stores in the United States and several European countries, marking their first appearance since the device's release in March. These refurbished models offer approximately a 15% discount compared to new prices, with an 11-inch M3 iPad Air (128GB, Wi-Fi) for example, priced at $509 instead of $599. All refurbished iPads include a new battery and outer shell, come with accessories, and are backed by Apple's one-year warranty, ensuring a like-new condition after thorough inspection.
