MMCTAgent: Enabling multimodal reasoning over large video and image collections

Modern multimodal AI models struggle with complex real-world reasoning over long-form videos and large image collections due to limitations in temporal context and iterative refinement. To address this, Microsoft Research developed MMCTAgent, a Multi-modal Critical Thinking Agent built on AutoGen with a Planner-Critic architecture. This system leverages modality-specific agents and tool-based iterative reasoning, significantly enhancing performance, explainability, and scalability for analyzing complex queries across diverse visual data.
