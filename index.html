Tell me when: Building agents that can wait, monitor, and act

Modern LLM agents struggle with long-running monitoring tasks like tracking emails or price drops because they lack the ability to know *when* to check, leading to resource waste or failure. To address this, Microsoft Research introduced SentinelStep, a mechanism that allows agents to patiently monitor conditions for hours or days by dynamically adjusting polling intervals and managing context. Implemented in their Magentic-UI system, SentinelStep significantly improves the reliability of agents on these extended tasks, enabling more proactive and efficient AI assistants.

OpenAI Acquires Apple Shortcuts Creators to Bring Deep Mac Integration to ChatGPT

OpenAI has acquired Software Applications Incorporated and its AI app, Sky, which was founded by former Apple employees known for creating the popular Workflow app and contributing to Apple's Shortcuts feature. Sky is a Mac-based natural language AI assistant capable of answering questions and performing tasks across open Mac windows. OpenAI plans to integrate Sky's deep macOS integration into ChatGPT and has brought the entire Sky team, including its founders, onboard.
