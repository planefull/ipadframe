Ideas: More AI-resilient biosecurity with the Paraphrase Project

The Microsoft Research "Paraphrase Project" exposed a critical vulnerability: AI tools could be used to redesign toxic proteins, like ricin, in ways that evaded existing biosecurity screening systems, essentially creating a "zero-day" threat. A cross-sector team developed and deployed "patches" to strengthen these screening tools, demonstrating that AI-resilient solutions are possible and need continuous updates. This effort also pioneered a "perpetual gatekeeping" strategy for responsible data sharing, offering a model for navigating information hazards in dual-use technologies like AI and synthetic biology.

Apple Remains the World's Most Valuable Brand in 2025

Apple has retained its position as the world's most valuable brand for the 13th consecutive year, topping Interbrand's 2025 ranking with an estimated brand value of $470.9 billion, despite a 4% decline. The annual Interbrand report evaluates companies on financial performance and brand strength, noting a 4.4% year-over-year increase in the combined value of the top 100 brands. While Microsoft secured second place, the report also highlighted AI chipmaker Nvidia's record 116% surge in brand value, moving from 36th to 15th, alongside declines for Nike and Tesla.
