Using AI to assist in rare disease diagnosis

Genetic professionals struggle to diagnose rare diseases from whole genome sequencing data due to information overload, inefficient collaboration, and difficulties prioritizing reanalysis, leading to low diagnostic rates. To address these challenges, Microsoft Research, Drexel University, and the Broad Institute developed and studied a generative AI assistant designed to streamline genetic data analysis. This AI tool aims to improve diagnostic yield and reduce diagnosis time by flagging cases for reanalysis based on new scientific findings and efficiently synthesizing crucial gene and variant information, fostering a collaborative human-AI approach.

Algoriddim's Djay App Adds Spotify Integration on Mac

Algoriddim's djay apps for Mac and Windows are being updated with new Spotify integration, allowing Spotify Premium subscribers to access the entire music catalog directly within the software. Users can now mix their own libraries, playlists, and Spotify's curated content, utilizing features like Automix for seamless transitions in 51 markets. This expansion significantly broadens djay's reach beyond its existing Apple Music support, making the free-to-download app (with an optional Pro subscription) more versatile for a larger DJ user base.
