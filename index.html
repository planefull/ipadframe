When industry knowledge meets PIKE-RAG: The innovation behind Signify’s customer service boost

Signify, a global leader in connected LED lighting, faced significant challenges in providing accurate and efficient answers to complex technical questions from professional users, due to a vast array of product models and fragmented documentation. To address this, Signify collaborated with Microsoft Research Asia to implement PIKE-RAG technology into their knowledge management system on Microsoft Azure. This integration resulted in a 12% improvement in answer accuracy by effectively parsing multimodal documents, eliminating reliance on erroneous data sources, and performing dynamic multi-hop reasoning for complex inquiries.
