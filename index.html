Using AI to assist in rare disease diagnosis

Diagnosing rare genetic diseases is a complex and time-consuming process for genetic professionals due to the vast amounts of whole genome sequencing data requiring extensive sensemaking. Microsoft Research, in collaboration with Drexel University and the Broad Institute, developed a generative AI assistant to address these challenges. This AI-powered tool aims to streamline workflows by flagging cases for reanalysis and synthesizing crucial gene and variant information, ultimately improving diagnostic yield and patient outcomes.
