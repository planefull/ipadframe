Applicability vs. job displacement: further notes on our recent research on AI and occupations

Microsoft Research clarified its paper, "Working with AI: Measuring the Occupational Implications of Generative AI," which explored how AI chatbots like Copilot could assist various occupations, particularly in knowledge work and communication tasks. The authors explicitly stated that their research identifies AI's usefulness in performing subtasks, not its ability to eliminate entire jobs, correcting public misinterpretations. They also highlighted methodological limitations, acknowledging that job complexity extends beyond individual tasks and advocating for AI as a tool to complement human strengths.
