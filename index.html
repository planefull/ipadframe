Tell me when: Building agents that can wait, monitor, and act

Modern LLM agents excel at complex tasks but fail at simple monitoring activities due to their inability to intelligently manage polling frequency and context over time. To address this, Microsoft Research introduced SentinelStep, a mechanism that wraps agents in a workflow with dynamic polling and careful context management. This enables agents to efficiently monitor conditions for hours or days, significantly improving reliability for long-running tasks like tracking emails or price changes, and is open-sourced as part of Magentic-UI.

Samsung's Galaxy XR Mixed Reality Headset Now Available for $1,800

Samsung has launched its new Galaxy XR mixed reality headset, priced at $1,800, positioning it as a more affordable alternative to Apple's Vision Pro while being more expensive than Meta Quest devices. The headset emphasizes a "human-centric design" for comfort, featuring a lighter weight, pressure-distributing frame, 4K micro-OLED screens, and advanced tracking cameras powered by a Snapdragon XR2+ Gen 2 chip. Running on the Android XR platform with integrated Google Gemini AI, it offers diverse functionalities including navigation, content discovery, real-time object information, spatial media experiences, and AI-assisted XR gaming.
