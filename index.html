Using AI to assist in rare disease diagnosis

Microsoft Research, in collaboration with Drexel University and the Broad Institute, conducted a study on integrating generative AI to assist genetic professionals in diagnosing rare diseases through whole genome sequencing. The research identified challenges like information overload and reanalysis prioritization, proposing an AI assistant to synthesize biomedical data, flag cases for reanalysis based on new findings, and aggregate scientific literature. This AI-enhanced approach aims to streamline complex data analysis, improve diagnostic rates for rare genetic diseases, and ultimately enhance patient outcomes by facilitating better human-AI collaboration.

iPhone 17e: All the Rumors So Far

Apple is anticipated to release the budget-friendly iPhone 17e in early 2026, continuing its new "e" series as a successor to the iPhone 16e and establishing an annual update strategy. Rumors indicate it will feature the powerful A19 chip, similar to the standard iPhone 17, but will likely reuse the iPhone 16e's OLED display panel from the iPhone 14.
