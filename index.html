When industry knowledge meets PIKE-RAG: The innovation behind Signify’s customer service boost

Signify, a leading LED lighting company, faced challenges providing accurate and efficient answers to professional users due to complex product data and diverse documentation. To address this, they partnered with Microsoft Research Asia to integrate PIKE-RAG technology into their knowledge management system on Microsoft Azure. This advanced RAG system improved answer accuracy by 12% through multimodal document parsing, direct use of original data sources, and dynamic multi-hop reasoning, showcasing its strong generalization capabilities for industrial knowledge.

OLED Display Coming to M6 Pro/Max MacBook Pro, Not Base M6 Model

Apple will initially reserve its OLED display upgrade for the high-end 14-inch and 16-inch MacBook Pro models featuring M6 Pro and M6 Max chips. The base 14-inch M6 MacBook Pro, however, will continue to utilize a mini-LED screen. This information, reported by Bloomberg's Mark Gurman, suggests the redesigned OLED machines will also have a thinner chassis and touch support, expected for release in late 2026 or early 2027.
