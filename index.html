Tell me when: Building agents that can wait, monitor, and act

Current LLM agents struggle with long-running monitoring tasks, such as tracking emails or price changes, because they lack the ability to know *when* to check, leading to inefficiency or failure. Microsoft Research introduces "SentinelStep," a new mechanism that enables agents to perform these persistent monitoring tasks by wrapping them in a workflow with dynamic polling and careful context management. Integrated into Magentic-UI, SentinelStep significantly improves agent reliability for extended durations by intelligently adjusting polling intervals and preventing context overflow, making agents more patient and proactive.
