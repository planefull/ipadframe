Tell me when: Building agents that can wait, monitor, and act

Modern LLM agents struggle with long-running monitoring tasks, such as tracking emails or price changes, because they lack the ability to intelligently determine *when* to check, leading to inefficiency or failure. To address this, Microsoft Research introduces SentinelStep, a mechanism that enables agents to complete these tasks by dynamically adjusting polling frequency and managing context to prevent overflow over hours or days. Implemented in the Magentic-UI prototype and validated with the new SentinelBench evaluation suite, SentinelStep significantly improves agent reliability for extended monitoring, laying the groundwork for more patient and proactive AI assistants.

Canva Relaunches Affinity as Free All-in-One Design App

Canva has relaunched its Affinity design suite, consolidating its previously separate vector, photo, and layout tools into a single, free app for Mac and Windows. The new "Affinity by Canva" unifies these capabilities into one interface but now requires a free Canva account, with premium subscribers gaining access to AI tools. This strategic move aims to rival Adobe by offering a robust free core app, though its freemium approach and mandatory Canva integration represent a significant change for existing users.
