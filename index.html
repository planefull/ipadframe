When to Expect the iPhone Air 2 and 20th Anniversary iPhone With Camera Under Screen

According to Bloomberg's Mark Gurman, Apple plans to release a second-generation iPhone Air around March 2027, followed by a 20th-anniversary iPhone in September 2027. The anniversary iPhone is expected to feature a curved glass screen with a hidden under-screen front camera, while the iPhone Air 2 may include a 2nm chip, larger battery, and vapor chamber cooling. Gurman indicates the iPhone Air 2 was always slated for 2027 and suggests Apple might proceed with it despite reportedly low demand for the current iPhone Air model.
