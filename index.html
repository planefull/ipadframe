Ideas: More AI-resilient biosecurity with the Paraphrase Project

The Microsoft Research Podcast explores the Paraphrase Project, which demonstrated how open-source AI tools could "paraphrase" toxic protein sequences, allowing them to evade existing biosecurity screening systems. This discovery, deemed the first "zero day" in AI and biosecurity, spurred a collaborative effort involving Microsoft, Twist Bioscience, and IBBIS to develop and implement more robust screening solutions. The project successfully enhanced global biosecurity measures and established a novel "perpetual gatekeeping" approach for responsible data and software sharing to mitigate information hazards in dual-use technologies.

iOS 26 Liquid Glass Design Copied by Android Smartphone Maker

Chinese smartphone maker Vivo has unveiled its latest operating system, OriginOS 6, which heavily borrows from Apple's "Liquid Glass" design language found in iOS 26. The new OS features a striking resemblance to iOS 26, incorporating rounded, translucent glass-like buttons, squircle icons, translucent app folders, docks, notifications, and wallpapers with 2D to 3D shifting effects. Despite Vivo's marketing referencing water and flow, the design influence from Apple's interface is unmistakable, making it easily mistaken for iOS 26.
