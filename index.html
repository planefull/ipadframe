Using AI to assist in rare disease diagnosis

Genetic professionals face significant challenges in diagnosing rare genetic diseases due to information overload, difficulties in collaborative data sharing, and the complexity of reanalyzing whole genome sequencing data. Microsoft Research, in collaboration with Drexel University and the Broad Institute, conducted a study to design a generative AI-based assistant to support these professionals' sensemaking tasks. The proposed AI tool aims to streamline diagnosis by flagging cases for reanalysis based on new scientific findings and efficiently synthesizing vast amounts of gene and variant data, ultimately improving diagnostic yield and reducing the time to diagnosis for patients.

Amazon Unveils New Echo Speakers with Alexa+ AI and Omnisense Technology

Amazon has launched new Echo Dot Max, Echo Studio, Echo Show 8, and Echo Show 11 smart speakers, all powered by the new Alexa+ AI assistant. These devices feature faster chips with AI acceleration and a custom Omnisense sensor platform for ambient AI capabilities, enabling personalized reminders and home alerts. The updated speakers offer enhanced audio and the Echo Show models boast improved displays and cameras, with all devices available for pre-order and designed to compete with Apple's HomePod line.
