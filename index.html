Ideas: More AI-resilient biosecurity with the Paraphrase Project

The "Paraphrase Project," featured on the Microsoft Research Podcast, uncovered a critical vulnerability where AI tools could redesign toxic proteins to bypass existing biosecurity screening systems, dubbed the first "zero-day" in AI and biosecurity. A cross-sector team, including Microsoft, Twist Bioscience, and IBBIS, developed and disseminated robust screening updates to address these AI-generated threats, strengthening global biosecurity. This pioneering effort also established a novel "perpetual gatekeeping" model for responsible data and software sharing, setting a precedent for managing information hazards in other dual-use technologies.
