MMCTAgent: Enabling multimodal reasoning over large video and image collections

Modern multimodal AI models struggle with complex reasoning over long-form videos and large image collections, due to limitations in temporal context and iterative processing. To address this, Microsoft Research developed MMCTAgent (Multi-modal Critical Thinking Agent), built on AutoGen with a Planner–Critic architecture, which enables structured, iterative reasoning across various visual modalities. MMCTAgent, featuring specialized ImageAgent and VideoAgent tools, significantly enhances performance on benchmark datasets by providing explainable, extensible, and scalable insights for complex visual question answering.
