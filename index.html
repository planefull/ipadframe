When industry knowledge meets PIKE-RAG: The innovation behind Signify’s customer service boost

Signify, a global leader in LED lighting, faced significant challenges in efficiently providing accurate technical answers from its vast and complex product documentation to professional users. To improve this, Signify partnered with Microsoft Research Asia to integrate their advanced PIKE-RAG technology into its Microsoft Azure-based knowledge management system. This specialized RAG system, which excels at multimodal document parsing, multi-hop reasoning, and domain adaptation, led to a 12% improvement in answer accuracy for Signify, highlighting its potential for generalization across various industries.
