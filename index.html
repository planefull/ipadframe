Applicability vs. job displacement: further notes on our recent research on AI and occupations

Microsoft Research clarified its paper, "Working with AI," emphasizing that its purpose is to identify occupations where AI chatbots can be useful as tools, rather than to predict job elimination. The research, based on Bing Copilot usage, found AI most beneficial for knowledge work and communication tasks like writing and information gathering. The authors stress that the study does not suggest job replacement, acknowledging methodological limitations and the nuanced role of AI in complementing human strengths.
