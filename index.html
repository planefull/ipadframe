Apple Exploring Touch-Sensitive Layers Built Into Official iPhone Cases

Apple is reportedly developing official iPhone cases that would function as secondary touch interfaces for future devices. These cases, detailed in a 2024 Apple patent, would integrate touch-sensitive layers to allow users to control phone functions through gestures and taps, potentially replacing physical buttons and even incorporating biometric input. This technology could be particularly relevant for a rumored future bezel-free iPhone, providing necessary controls and ergonomic surfaces that an all-screen design might otherwise lack.
