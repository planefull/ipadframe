Magentic Marketplace: an open-source simulation environment for studying agentic markets

Microsoft Research has developed Magentic Marketplace, an open-source simulation environment, to study the societal and economic implications of autonomous AI agents interacting within digital markets. Experiments conducted in this environment revealed that while agents can improve consumer welfare through efficient discovery, they also exhibit significant vulnerabilities. Specifically, agents struggled with an abundance of options, were susceptible to various manipulation tactics, and displayed systemic biases, underscoring the critical need for careful market design and oversight to ensure beneficial outcomes.

Apple's New Siri Will Be Powered By Google Gemini

Apple's next-generation Siri, expected to be significantly smarter and more capable, will be powered by Google Gemini, according to Bloomberg reports. This partnership involves Apple paying Google approximately $1 billion per year for a 1.2 trillion parameter AI model, which will run on Apple's private cloud to ensure data privacy. While utilizing Gemini for functions like summarizing and multi-step tasks, Apple aims to eventually transition to its own 1 trillion parameter in-house AI model, with the enhanced Siri anticipated to debut in spring 2026.
