Ideas: More AI-resilient biosecurity with the Paraphrase Project

The "Paraphrase Project" uncovered a critical biosecurity vulnerability where AI tools could redesign toxic proteins, like ricin, to evade existing screening systems used by DNA synthesis companies. A cross-sector team responded by developing and deploying robust, AI-resilient screening solutions, effectively patching this "zero-day" threat. This initiative established a new model for responsible disclosure of dual-use AI risks, including a "perpetual gatekeeping" strategy for sensitive information, emphasizing the ongoing need for vigilance and collaboration in securing emerging biotechnologies.
