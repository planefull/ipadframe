Ideas: More AI-resilient biosecurity with the Paraphrase Project

The Microsoft Research podcast *Ideas* discusses the "Paraphrase Project," which explored the dual-use nature of AI in protein design and its implications for biosecurity. Researchers demonstrated that open-source AI tools could "paraphrase" toxic protein sequences, making them undetectable by existing biosecurity screening systems at DNA synthesis companies. This led to a collaborative, cybersecurity-style "red-teaming" effort that developed and shared more robust screening solutions and established a novel approach for responsible disclosure of sensitive scientific findings.

iOS 26: Make Your iPhone Wait on Hold for You

iOS 26 introduces Hold Assist, a new feature that allows your iPhone to wait on hold for you during phone calls. It automatically detects hold music and then alerts you when a live person comes on the line, letting you use your device for other tasks in the interim. Hold Assist can be activated automatically after a brief delay or manually, and is available on all iPhones supporting iOS 26 without any setup required.
