Tell me when: Building agents that can wait, monitor, and act

Modern LLM agents struggle with long-running monitoring tasks, failing to efficiently track conditions over time due to issues with knowing *when* to check and managing context. To address this, Microsoft Research developed SentinelStep, a mechanism that enables agents to complete these tasks by dynamically adjusting polling intervals and carefully managing agent state to prevent context overflow. Integrated into Magentic-UI, SentinelStep significantly improves the reliability of agents for extended monitoring periods, laying the groundwork for more patient, proactive, and efficient AI assistants.

iOS 26.1 Features: Everything New in iOS 26.1

iOS 26.1, the first major update to iOS 26, is now available, introducing useful new features and addressing prior complaints. Key improvements include a Liquid Glass transparency toggle, the ability to disable the accidental Lock Screen camera swipe, and a "slide to stop" function for alarms. Additionally, the update expands Apple Intelligence and AirPods Live Translation language support, adds custom workouts to the Fitness app, and reintroduces Slide Over multitasking for iPadOS.
