Using AI to assist in rare disease diagnosis

Diagnosing rare genetic diseases is highly challenging due to the vast and complex whole genome sequencing data, leading to information overload, collaboration difficulties, and inefficient reanalysis processes. To address this, Microsoft Research, Drexel University, and the Broad Institute co-designed a generative AI assistant to support genetic professionals in their sensemaking tasks. This AI tool is envisioned to flag cases for reanalysis based on new scientific findings and synthesize critical gene and variant information, ultimately aiming to increase diagnostic yield and shorten diagnosis times through enhanced human-AI collaboration.

10+ macOS Tahoe Features You Might Have Missed

macOS Tahoe introduces many impactful features beyond its new Liquid Glass design, significantly enhancing the user experience. Additionally, the update brings synced Live Activities, improved Safari Picture-in-Picture, and gaming-focused features like an optimized Low Power Mode and Game Overlay, all aimed at making the Mac more versatile and productive.
