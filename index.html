Tell me when: Building agents that can wait, monitor, and act

Modern LLM agents struggle with long-running monitoring tasks, like watching for price drops or email responses, because they lack the ability to efficiently manage polling frequency and context over extended periods. To address this, Microsoft Research introduces SentinelStep, a mechanism within their Magentic-UI prototype that enables agents to patiently monitor conditions for hours or days. SentinelStep achieves this by employing dynamic polling intervals and smart context management, significantly improving task reliability on longer durations and laying the groundwork for more proactive and efficient AI assistants.
