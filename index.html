Using AI to assist in rare disease diagnosis

Diagnosing rare genetic diseases is challenging due to the vast and complex data from whole genome sequencing, leading to low initial diagnosis rates and lengthy reanalysis processes. To address this, Microsoft Research, in collaboration with Drexel University and the Broad Institute, co-designed a generative AI-based assistant. This AI tool aims to streamline diagnosis by helping genetic professionals synthesize information, flag cases for reanalysis based on new scientific findings, and facilitate collaborative sensemaking, ultimately improving diagnostic yield and shortening diagnosis time for patients.

10+ macOS Tahoe Features You Might Have Missed

macOS Tahoe introduces a variety of useful, yet less obvious features that significantly enhance the user experience beyond its new Liquid Glass design. Key improvements include extensive customization options for the Menu Bar and Lock Screen, along with enhanced Spotlight capabilities such as clipboard history, streamlined search filters, and customizable Quick Keys. Additionally, the update brings iOS-style automations, seamless Live Activities syncing with iPhones, improved Safari Picture-in-Picture controls, and new gaming features like an optimized Low Power Mode and a Game Overlay.
