Tell me when: Building agents that can wait, monitor, and act

Modern LLM agents struggle with long-running monitoring tasks, such as tracking emails or price changes, because they lack the ability to intelligently determine *when* to check for updates, leading to either early abandonment or excessive resource use. To solve this, Microsoft Research introduced SentinelStep, a mechanism integrated into their Magentic-UI agentic system, which enables agents to complete these tasks through dynamic polling and careful context management. This approach significantly improves the reliability of agents over extended periods, making them capable of patiently monitoring conditions for hours or days while remaining efficient and proactive.
