Applicability vs. job displacement: further notes on our recent research on AI and occupations

This article clarifies previous research, explaining that their study identified occupations where generative AI chatbots could be useful for specific tasks, especially in knowledge work and communication. The authors emphasize that their research does not conclude that AI will eliminate jobs, explicitly cautioning against such interpretations. Instead, the paper highlights AI's potential as a tool to assist with subtasks, while acknowledging the limitations of focusing solely on tasks and user queries.
