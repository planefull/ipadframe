Applicability vs. job displacement: further notes on our recent research on AI and occupations

Microsoft Research clarified its recent paper on AI and occupations, stating it identified generative AI as most useful for assisting with knowledge work and communication tasks like writing and information gathering across various jobs. The study explicitly did not conclude that AI would eliminate jobs, cautioning against misinterpretations of its findings on task applicability. Researchers emphasized that their methodology, which maps user queries to the O*NET database, does not capture the full complexity and nuance of occupations, positioning AI as a tool to complement human strengths rather than replace roles.
