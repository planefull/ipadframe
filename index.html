Using AI to assist in rare disease diagnosis

Genetic professionals face significant challenges, including information overload and complex data synthesis, when diagnosing rare diseases through whole genome sequencing, leading to low diagnosis rates. Microsoft Research, in collaboration with Drexel University and the Broad Institute, conducted a study to explore how generative AI can support these professionals. They co-designed an AI assistant prototype designed to streamline workflows by flagging cases for reanalysis and synthesizing vast amounts of gene and variant information, ultimately aiming to improve diagnostic yield and reduce the time to diagnosis for rare genetic diseases.

Logitech Launches Solar-Powered Signature Slim K980 Keyboard

Logitech has introduced the Signature Slim Solar+ K980, a new full-size wireless keyboard that relies entirely on solar power, eliminating the need for charging cables. Utilizing LightCharge technology, it maintains power from ambient light and can operate for up to four months in complete darkness when fully charged, with an internal battery expected to last 10 years. This $99.99 eco-friendly keyboard offers a standard layout, Bluetooth connectivity for three devices, an AI Launch key, and compatibility with both Windows and macOS.
