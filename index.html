Ideas: More AI-resilient biosecurity with the Paraphrase Project

The "Paraphrase Project" explores the dual-use nature of AI in protein design, demonstrating how open-source AI tools can be used to generate toxic protein variants that evade existing biosecurity screening systems. A cross-sector team uncovered this "zero day" vulnerability, leading to a cybersecurity-style response to develop and implement more robust screening solutions. This effort not only strengthened global biosecurity by patching these vulnerabilities but also established a novel "perpetual gatekeeping" approach for responsible data sharing in sensitive research.

When Will Apple's Macs Get M5 Chips? 2025-2026 Launch Timeline

Apple's next-generation M5 silicon chip is expected to kick off a new wave of Mac refreshes, with some initial products like an iPad Pro and possibly a 14-inch MacBook Pro making an appearance in late 2025. Most Mac updates, including the MacBook Air, iMac, and Mac mini, are projected for early to mid-2026.
