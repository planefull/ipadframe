Tool-space interference in the MCP era: Designing for agent compatibility at scale

The rapid growth of agentic AI and the Model Context Protocol (MCP) is leading to a "society of agents" where diverse tools and agents from different developers must cooperate. While MCP enables powerful horizontal integration, a survey reveals significant "tool-space interference" issues, including excessive tool counts, overly long responses, complex parameter schemas, naming collisions, and poor error messages, which collectively reduce agent effectiveness. To address these challenges, the article recommends implementing formal namespaces, enhancing protocol support for client-provided resources, encouraging transparent server documentation, and developing client-side optimizations for a more compatible and scalable AI ecosystem.

iPhone Air vs. iPhone 17 Pro Max Battery Life Compared in New Test

Tom's Guide tested the battery life of the new iPhone Air, iPhone 17, iPhone 17 Pro, and iPhone 17 Pro Max with continuous 5G web browsing. The iPhone 17 Pro Max lasted the longest at 17 hours and 54 minutes, significantly outperforming the iPhone Air, which managed 12 hours and 2 minutes. These findings, which largely align with Apple's official figures, help set expectations for battery performance, despite not reflecting typical daily usage.
