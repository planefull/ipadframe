Ideas: More AI-resilient biosecurity with the Paraphrase Project

The Paraphrase Project uncovered a critical "zero-day" vulnerability where AI tools could redesign toxic proteins, such as ricin, to bypass existing biosecurity screening systems. In response, a cross-sector team developed and deployed "patches"—more robust screening methods for DNA synthesis companies—to strengthen defenses against AI-generated biological threats. This effort not only enhanced global biosecurity but also pioneered a novel "perpetual gatekeeping" approach for responsible data and software sharing, addressing the dual-use dilemma of emerging technologies.
