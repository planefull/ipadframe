Satechi Launches New 7-in-1 Multiport Adapter

Satechi has launched the new OntheGo 7-in-1 Multiport Adapter, a pocket-sized device ideal for travel, offering seven ports in a compact design. This adapter features a USB-C port with 100W passthrough charging, HDMI 4K/60Hz, two USB-A ports, SD/microSD card readers, and Gigabit Ethernet, alongside a magnetic attachment for MagSafe iPhones or iPad cases. Compatible with various devices including iPhones, iPads, Macs, and PCs, the adapter is available for $60.
