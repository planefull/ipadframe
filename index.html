Tell me when: Building agents that can wait, monitor, and act

Modern LLM agents struggle with long-running monitoring tasks, such as tracking emails or price changes, because they lack the ability to know *when* to check, leading to inefficiency or failure. To address this, Microsoft Research introduces SentinelStep, a mechanism within their Magentic-UI agentic system designed to enable agents to patiently monitor conditions for extended periods. SentinelStep achieves this by dynamically adjusting polling intervals and carefully managing context to prevent overflow, significantly improving the reliability of agents on tasks lasting hours or days.

Apple Invites Users to Design Digital Christmas Trees for Battersea Power Station Display

Apple has launched "Your Tree on Battersea," a campaign inviting people across the UK to design digital Christmas trees using an iPad. Winning artworks, submitted by November 23, 2025, will be projected onto the iconic Battersea Power Station chimneys in London throughout December. Participants can receive assistance through free "Today at Apple" sessions, and their designs will be showcased alongside artwork from public figures like Sir Stephen Fry.
