Tool-space interference in the MCP era: Designing for agent compatibility at scale

The rapid rise of agentic AI and the Model Context Protocol (MCP) is leading to a "society of agents" relying on horizontally integrated tools from various developers. However, this diversity creates "tool-space interference," causing issues like tool name collisions, overly large tool sets, excessively long responses, and ambiguous error messages, which diminish overall agent effectiveness. To mitigate these challenges, the article recommends formal namespaces, improved resource sharing protocols, transparent server documentation, client-side optimizations, and marketplace standards to ensure compatibility and efficiency in this evolving ecosystem.

iPhone 17: Using the New Dual Capture Video Feature

The iPhone 17 lineup introduces a new Dual Capture video feature, enabling simultaneous recording from both front and rear cameras in a picture-in-picture format. This is ideal for reaction videos and tutorials, capturing both the user and the scene, and can be easily activated within the Camera app's video settings. While recording, users can move the front camera window and switch lenses on Pro models, though it's limited to a single layout (rear dominant) and saves as one combined video file.
