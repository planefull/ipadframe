Ideas: More AI-resilient biosecurity with the Paraphrase Project

The "Paraphrase Project" identified a critical biosecurity vulnerability where AI tools could redesign toxic protein sequences to bypass existing screening systems, dubbed the "first zero day" in AI biosecurity. Through a collaborative "red-teaming" effort, the project developed and deployed robust new screening methods (patches) and a novel "perpetual gatekeeping" strategy for sensitive information, which were shared globally. This initiative establishes a crucial model for proactively addressing the dual-use challenges of AI in biology, emphasizing ongoing vigilance, responsible disclosure, and a layered defense against evolving threats.

Apple TV and Peacock Launch $15 Monthly Streaming Bundle, a 30% Discount

Apple and NBCUniversal are introducing a new streaming bundle, launching October 20, that combines Apple TV and Peacock for $15 per month. This offers customers a 30% discount compared to purchasing the $12.99 Apple TV and $10.99 Peacock Premium subscriptions separately. Additionally, Apple One subscribers can receive a 35% discount on Peacock Premium Plus, and both services will feature free sample episodes from each other's content within their apps.
