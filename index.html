Using AI to assist in rare disease diagnosis

Diagnosing rare genetic diseases is a complex and time-consuming process, hampered by the vast amounts of whole genome sequencing data and challenges in synthesizing information and reanalyzing unsolved cases. Microsoft Research, in collaboration with Drexel University and the Broad Institute, developed a generative AI-based assistant designed to support genetic professionals in these intensive sensemaking tasks. This AI tool aims to streamline analysis by flagging cases for reanalysis and synthesizing crucial gene and variant data, ultimately improving diagnostic yield and shortening the time to diagnosis for patients.

Apple Releases iOS 26.0.1 With Fixes for Wi-Fi, Cellular, and Camera Issues on iPhone 17 Models

Apple has released iOS 26.0.1 and iPadOS 26.0.1, important bug fix updates following the recent launch of iOS 26 and iPadOS 26. These updates specifically address critical issues for iPhone 17, iPhone Air, and iPhone 17 Pro models, including camera aberrations, ongoing cellular and Wi-Fi connectivity problems, and a tinted app icon bug. Users can download the new software over-the-air, which also fixes VoiceOver issues on both platforms and a floating keyboard bug on iPads.
