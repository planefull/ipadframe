Tool-space interference in the MCP era: Designing for agent compatibility at scale

The rise of agentic AI and the Model Context Protocol (MCP) is leading to a "society of agents" and "tools," shifting from vertically integrated systems to horizontally integrated ones. This new paradigm, however, introduces "tool-space interference," degrading agent performance through challenges like excessive tool counts, lengthy responses, complex parameters, and naming ambiguities. To ensure a scalable and efficient AI agent ecosystem, the article recommends solutions such as formal namespaces, improved client-provided resource handling, transparent server documentation, and client-side optimizations.

AirPods Pro 3 Use Acoustic Seal Test for Optimizing Sound Quality and ANC

The new AirPods Pro 3 introduce an Acoustic Seal Test, an updated process from previous models, to help users find the optimal ear tip fit for enhanced sound quality and noise cancellation.
