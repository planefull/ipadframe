Tell me when: Building agents that can wait, monitor, and act

Modern LLM agents struggle with long-running monitoring tasks like tracking emails or price changes because they lack the ability to know *when* to check, leading to inefficiency or failure. To address this, Microsoft Research introduced SentinelStep, a mechanism that wraps agents in a workflow with dynamic polling and careful context management, enabling them to monitor conditions for extended periods without getting sidetracked. Integrated into Magentic-UI and open-sourced, SentinelStep significantly improves agent reliability for longer tasks, laying the groundwork for more patient, proactive, and efficient AI assistants.

New Version of Siri to 'Lean' on Google Gemini

According to Mark Gurman's "Power On" newsletter, Apple plans to roll out a revamped Siri around March next year, accompanied by new smart home devices, an Apple TV, and a HomePod mini. This new Siri will "lean" on Google's Gemini for AI-powered web search, with Apple paying Google for a custom, privately run Gemini model to power its features. However, Gurman cautioned about potential user acceptance and brand challenges, while Apple Intelligence's launch in China continues to face regulatory issues.
