Ideas: More AI-resilient biosecurity with the Paraphrase Project

The Microsoft Research "Paraphrase Project" highlights the dual-use dilemma of AI in protein design, demonstrating how AI tools can be used to redesign toxic proteins that evade current biosecurity screening systems. A cross-sector team identified this "zero day" vulnerability, prompting the development and global dissemination of more robust, AI-resilient screening solutions, akin to cybersecurity patches. The project also pioneered a novel "perpetual gatekeeping" approach for responsibly sharing sensitive research data, addressing information hazards while advancing scientific transparency in dual-use fields.

Some Los Angeles Lakers Games Will Get 3D Live Streams on Vision Pro

Selected Los Angeles Lakers games from the 2025-26 season will be live-streamed in Apple's Immersive format on the Apple Vision Pro, offering viewers a 3D courtside experience. These 180-degree 8K recordings with Spatial Audio will be available through the Spectrum SportsNet app starting early next year, requiring a Spectrum internet or SportsNet+ subscription and visionOS 26. The immersive footage, captured with Blackmagic's new URSA Cine Immersive Live camera, will also have full game replays and highlights available on demand.
