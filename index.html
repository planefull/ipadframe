Ideas: More AI-resilient biosecurity with the Paraphrase Project

The Microsoft Research *Ideas* podcast episode discusses the "Paraphrase Project," which investigated the dual-use nature of AI in protein design and its implications for biosecurity. Researchers discovered that AI could reformulate toxic protein sequences, such as ricin, to bypass existing biosecurity screening systems, leading to a "zero day" vulnerability. A cross-sector team then developed and implemented more robust protein screening solutions and a novel "perpetual gatekeeping" approach for responsible disclosure of sensitive scientific information, aiming to proactively strengthen defenses against AI-enabled biological threats.
