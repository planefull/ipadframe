Using AI to assist in rare disease diagnosis

Genetic professionals face significant challenges in diagnosing rare diseases due to the vast and complex whole genome sequencing data, information overload, and difficulties in prioritizing reanalysis of unsolved cases. To address this, Microsoft Research, in collaboration with Drexel University and the Broad Institute, co-designed a generative AI assistant to streamline the complex "sensemaking" tasks inherent in genetic analysis. This AI-enhanced tool aims to improve diagnostic yield and reduce diagnosis time by efficiently flagging cases for reanalysis based on new scientific findings and synthesizing crucial gene and variant information, fostering better expert-AI collaboration.

10+ macOS Tahoe Features You Might Have Missed

macOS Tahoe introduces a range of significant features beyond its new Liquid Glass design, aiming to enhance the user experience and productivity. Key additions include extensive customization options for the menu bar and lock screen, powerful Spotlight improvements like clipboard history, Quick Keys, and streamlined search filters, alongside new iOS-style automations. The update also brings cross-device Live Activities syncing, easier Safari Picture-in-Picture controls, and gaming-specific optimizations such as an improved Low Power Mode and a new Game Overlay.
