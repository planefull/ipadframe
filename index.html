Everything New in iOS 26.2 Beta 2

Apple has released the second beta of iOS 26.2 to developers, introducing several new features including a "Liquid Glass" design for the Measure app's level, an option to sort games by size, and updated menu animations. The update also provides more details on Enhanced Safety Alerts, which notify users of emergency events like earthquakes or floods, are on by default, and can use approximate location for improved delivery. This developer beta is available now, with a public beta expected soon and the final iOS 26.2 release anticipated in December.
