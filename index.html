Tell me when: Building agents that can wait, monitor, and act

Modern LLM agents struggle with long-running monitoring tasks, such as waiting for emails or price drops, because they lack the ability to intelligently determine *when* to check, leading to inefficiency or failure. To address this, Microsoft Research developed SentinelStep, a mechanism that enables agents to complete these tasks by wrapping them in a workflow with dynamic polling and careful context management. SentinelStep dynamically adjusts polling intervals and prevents context overflow, significantly improving task reliability over extended durations and laying the groundwork for more patient, proactive, and efficient AI assistants.

Samsung's Galaxy XR Mixed Reality Headset Now Available for $1,800

Samsung has launched the Galaxy XR, a new mixed reality headset priced at $1,800, positioning it as a more affordable competitor to the Apple Vision Pro. Emphasizing long-term comfort with its human-centric design and lighter 545-gram weight, the headset features 4K micro-OLED screens, a Snapdragon XR2+ Gen 2 chip, and multiple cameras for tracking and 3D media. Running on the Android XR platform developed with Google, the Galaxy XR integrates Gemini AI for advanced functions like spatial navigation, object information retrieval, 2D media spatialization, and real-time coaching in XR games.
